title: Phase-dependent modulation calibration
---

**Background:** Galactic cosmic ray modulation models rely on solar proxies to forecast flux, but their predictive performance often degrades during solar cycle transitions.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved need to determine if calibrating transport-based modulation models specifically for the ascending and descending phases of the solar cycle improves prediction accuracy, addressing the observed performance degradation during phase-transition intervals.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a fundamental limitation in non-stationary heliophysics forecasting, where generic proxy-based models fail to account for distinct physical regime behaviors.

**Evidence:** A phase-dependent calibration may therefore better capture the distinct modulation conditions characterizing different stages of the solar cycle.
