title: Higher-dimensional Latent Space Modeling
---

**Background:** The forecasting of high-resolution spatiotemporal environmental data often requires a trade-off between model accuracy and computational efficiency. While dimensionality reduction techniques like SVD facilitate this balance, the current approach relies on a simplified latent-space model.

**Question / Future Work:** The framework currently utilizes a low-dimensional latent space for forecasting, but there is a need to explore the performance gains and scalability of using higher-dimensional latent representations. Investigating these higher-dimensional spaces could better capture complex sub-grid physical phenomena that are lost during aggressive truncation.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the optimal dimensionality for latent-space models is critical for balancing the preservation of fine-scale spatiotemporal features with the computational benefits of dimensionality reduction.
