title: Solar PV Load Disaggregation
---

**Background:** Solar photovoltaic (PV) integration into residential energy systems complicates load forecasting because smart meters typically record net consumption, which obscures the underlying household load and local solar generation. Separating these components is essential for accurate demand side management.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop dual-output forecasting architectures capable of independently predicting grid consumption and solar generation to isolate the household load signal from solar generation confounding.

**Why It Matters:** Precise disaggregation is necessary for residential energy management systems to optimize battery dispatch and load scheduling without being misled by solar generation patterns.

**Evidence:** a principled separation of load and solar forecasting is preferable, and constitutes one of our proposed extensions.
