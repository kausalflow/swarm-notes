title: Foundation Model STG Integration

**Background:** Spatial-Temporal Graph (STG) forecasting requires capturing complex dependencies across high-dimensional nodes and long temporal horizons, which typically results in high memory and computational demands.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to bridge the gap between specialized, high-efficiency STG architectures and general-purpose time-series foundation models to improve cross-domain generalization and predictive robustness.

**Why It Matters:** Foundation models offer the potential to unify feature extraction and improve generalization, which is currently a major limitation of task-specific STG models.
