title: Integrating Scenarios and Densities
---

**Background:** Central banks currently utilize scenario analysis for narrative-based risk exploration and predictive distributions for probabilistic risk quantification, yet these methodologies typically operate in parallel without a unified framework.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to standardize protocols for integrating scenario-based narratives with probabilistic predictive densities into a cohesive decision-support framework that bridges the gap between judgmental design and econometric rigor.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing a formal, reproducible link between qualitative scenario narratives and quantitative risk metrics is critical for transparent policy deliberation.

**Evidence:** The historical survey reveals that these two approaches developed in parallel for distinct institutional and intellectual reasons.
