title: Steerable Interpretable Frequency Filters
---

**Background:** Transformer-based models often exhibit an inherent spectral bias toward low-frequency signals, which hinders their capacity to capture complex high-frequency temporal dynamics in time-series data.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing interpretable and steerable learnable frequency filters that can dynamically adjust spectral sensitivity—rather than relying on fixed or black-box learnable filters—remains an open challenge for improving the expressivity and spectral utilization of attention-based models.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing spectral bias is critical for enhancing transformer performance in high-frequency, complex-dynamic time-series tasks.
