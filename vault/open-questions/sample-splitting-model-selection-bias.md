title: Sample-splitting model selection bias
---

**Background:** Effective connectivity is often estimated from time-series data using data-adaptive model selection, which can violate the assumptions of classical inference procedures due to post-selection bias.

**Question / Future Work:** Development of rigorous model conditioning procedures that ensure a clear separation between outcome construction and causal inference when using adaptive selection methods.

**Why It Matters:** Addresses the fundamental challenge of ensuring valid inference when the outcome itself is derived via data-dependent processes.
