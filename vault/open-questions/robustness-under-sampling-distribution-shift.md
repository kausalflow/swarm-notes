title: Robustness under sampling distribution shift
---

**Background:** The performance of machine learning models in healthcare is frequently threatened by distribution shifts in clinical sampling protocols across institutions.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the resilience of LLM-based time series models when exposed to data collected under different clinical sampling policies and documentation frequencies remains an unresolved challenge for deployment.

**Why It Matters:** Sampling shift is a fundamental bottleneck for deploying predictive healthcare models, as 'informative missingness' models are highly sensitive to the underlying data-collection mechanism.

**Evidence:** Future work includes... studying robustness under sampling distribution shift, such as deployment across hospitals with different sampling policies.
