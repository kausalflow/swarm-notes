title: Scalable Adaptive Partitioning
---

**Background:** Data-adaptive partitions are used to approximate Perron–Frobenius operators for high-dimensional dynamical systems, but current strategies for creating these partitions face significant scalability challenges as dimensionality increases.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing partition strategies that are both scalable to high-dimensional state spaces and capable of maintaining accuracy for complex or non-equilibrium dynamics is a fundamental bottleneck for operator-theoretic system identification.

**Why It Matters:** Effective partition design is the primary constraint on scaling operator-theoretic methods to high-dimensional real-world forecasting problems.
