title: Robustness in Wild Deployment
---

**Background:** While wearable foundation models show potential for activity and gesture recognition, they are typically limited to fixed-vocabulary tasks and controlled environmental settings.

**Question / Future Work:** Focus on long-term robustness against sensor drift and increasing the gesture vocabulary to support diverse, real-world usage beyond stationary or controlled laboratory settings.

**Why It Matters:** Robustness to longitudinal drift and expanded gesture sets is essential for transitioning wearable interfaces from benchmarks to reliable everyday utilities.
