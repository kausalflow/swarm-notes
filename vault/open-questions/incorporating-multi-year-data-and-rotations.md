title: Multi-Year Data and Rotations
---

**Background:** Classification accuracy for farming systems can be affected by the inclusion of multi-year temporal context, especially since organic systems often feature distinct and longer-term crop rotation schedules.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should incorporate multi-year observations and crop rotation information to further strengthen classification performance and enhance the generalizability of organic/conventional farming system discrimination across different regions and production systems.
