title: Federated Generative Communication Efficiency
---

**Background:** Federated generative models often require numerous communication rounds to converge, posing a substantial deployment barrier in large-scale healthcare settings.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to optimize communication overhead for federated generative frameworks to ensure viability in distributed environments where communication is costly or latency-sensitive.

**Why It Matters:** Communication bandwidth is a primary constraint in practical federated learning, particularly when training complex generative models across multiple institutions.
