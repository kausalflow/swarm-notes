title: Rigorous nonlinear system analysis
---

**Background:** The analysis in the paper was rigorously performed for linear dynamical systems. However, many practical systems are nonlinear, though empirical evidence was provided suggesting similar qualitative behaviors might extend to nonlinear settings.

**Question / Future Work:** A rigorous theoretical treatment comparing the statistical efficiency of single-step versus multi-step predictors specifically for nonlinear dynamical systems, beyond the provided empirical evidence, is identified as future work.
