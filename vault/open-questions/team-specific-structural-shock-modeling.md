title: Team-Specific Structural Shock Modeling
---

**Background:** Football team strength is non-stationary and dynamic, with performance shifts frequently driven by events such as transfers, managerial changes, and squad turnover.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of team-specific or player-level models to quantify the impact of structural shocks—such as player transfers or coaching changes—remains an open challenge, especially regarding the heterogeneity of team responses.

**Why It Matters:** Improving the responsiveness of rating systems to structural shocks is critical for maintaining the accuracy of dynamic strength estimates in professional sports.

**Evidence:** Future work should develop team-specific or player-level structural shock models.
