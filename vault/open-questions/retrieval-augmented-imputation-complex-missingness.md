title: Retrieval for Complex Missingness
---

**Background:** Retrieval-augmented models for time series imputation frequently struggle with block-wise missingness and sustained sensor-level failures where data segments are systematically absent.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate retrieval-augmented frameworks capable of effectively handling complex, systematic data degradation scenarios, such as block-wise missing data, which extend beyond the random missingness patterns typically evaluated in current imputation literature.

**Why It Matters:** Real-world sensor systems experience sustained outages rather than just random point-wise errors, making robust imputation for systematic failure modes critical for reliability.

**Evidence:** We also aim to extend ALER-TI to more challenging missingness patterns, such as block missingness and sensor-level failures.
