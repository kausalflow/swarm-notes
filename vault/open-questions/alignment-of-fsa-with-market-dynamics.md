title: Aligning sentiment with markets
---

**Background:** Automated sentiment extraction from financial news remains largely evaluated through intrinsic NLP metrics, leaving the degree to which these signals genuinely align with broader financial market dynamics and long-run equilibrium behaviors under-explored.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to systematically bridge the gap between text-based sentiment indicators and quantitative financial time-series outcomes, specifically moving beyond simple accuracy metrics toward validating the causal or cointegrated relationship between semantic features and market regimes.

**Why It Matters:** Addresses a critical gap in financial forecasting where sentiment models are often decoupled from robust economic validation.
