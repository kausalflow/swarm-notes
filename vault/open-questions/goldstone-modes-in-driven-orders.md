title: Goldstone Mode Effects
---

**Background:** Theoretical treatments of ordered phases in many-body systems often involve approximations, such as the large-N saddle-point approximation used here, which may omit certain physical effects.

**Question / Future Work:** The potential effects of Goldstone modes on the ordered phases found using the large-N analysis need to be incorporated to provide a more complete description of the system's behavior.
