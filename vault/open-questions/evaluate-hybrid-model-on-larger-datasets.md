**Background:** Research involving hybrid quantum-classical machine learning models, particularly those using Variational Quantum Circuits (VQC) in small-cohort clinical settings, is preliminary.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should involve extending the evaluation of the hybrid quantum-classical forecasting model to larger and more diverse physiological datasets.
