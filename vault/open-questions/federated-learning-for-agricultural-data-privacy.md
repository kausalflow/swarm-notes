title: Federated Learning for Agricultural Privacy
---

**Background:** Data privacy concerns in agriculture hinder the sharing of localized data required for training robust, large-scale predictive models across different regions or farming businesses.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating federated learning as a mechanism to enable collaborative model training across disparate agricultural datasets while ensuring that sensitive, localized raw data remains private and is not directly shared between stakeholders.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical bottleneck for scaling agricultural AI models, as organizations are often reluctant to pool proprietary or sensitive field-level data.
