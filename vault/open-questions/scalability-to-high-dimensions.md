title: High-Dimensional Scalability Analysis
---

**Background:** The M-vine test is explicitly designed to accommodate multivariate Granger causality involving $d+1$ series, extending prior bivariate frameworks. The simulation study confirms the advantage of the joint test over pairwise tests in certain scenarios where individual causal links are weak.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the effect of increasing the number of conditioning series ($d$) on the power and size control of the M-vine test, particularly exploring the limits of reliable detection when many irrelevant conditioning variables are present in the multivariate setting.
