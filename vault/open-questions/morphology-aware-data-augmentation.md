title: Morphology-Aware Data Augmentation
---

**Background:** Data augmentation in time-series analysis often relies on global statistical transformations that can inadvertently destroy critical local morphological features like spikes or sharp bursts.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing augmentation techniques that preserve specific signal morphology (structural integrity) is essential for improving robustness without introducing artifacts that invalidate the physiological signal.

**Why It Matters:** As deep learning models rely increasingly on synthetic or augmented data for training, ensuring these processes maintain the signal's biological validity is a prerequisite for reliable clinical deployment.
