title: Feature Library Bias Mitigation
---

**Background:** The effectiveness of feature-based parameter inference for time series is constrained by the choice of summary statistics, which can introduce library-specific biases.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate methods to decouple the inferred low-dimensional manifold structure from the biases inherent in the specific composition of the chosen feature library to ensure extracted components reflect the underlying generative dynamics.

**Why It Matters:** Fundamental for ensuring the reliability and generalizability of model-free inference techniques across different scientific domains and varying data regimes.
