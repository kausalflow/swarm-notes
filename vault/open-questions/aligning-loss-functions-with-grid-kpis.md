title: Aligning Loss Functions with Grid KPIs
---

**Background:** Evaluation metrics for time-series forecasting often prioritize aggregate statistical accuracy over application-specific requirements, such as grid stability or asset overload prevention.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should focus on aligning the loss functions and optimization objectives of load forecasting models more closely with application-oriented metrics to directly optimize models for grid-relevant KPIs without sacrificing probabilistic accuracy.

**Why It Matters:** Aligning training objectives with utility-specific KPIs is crucial for transitioning research models into critical infrastructure applications.

**Evidence:** Future work could additionally examine how to adapt the loss function of load forecasting models to optimize with respect to the proposed application-oriented metric.
