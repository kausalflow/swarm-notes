**Background:** A novel plugin leverages spectral decomposition to augment large-scale, pretrained time series foundation models with frequency-aware dependency modeling.

**Question / Future Work:** Integrating spectral-aware dependency modeling into large-scale pretrained time series backbones is a promising direction to enhance their ability to generalize, adapt, and transfer knowledge to unseen environments with minimal fine-tuning.
