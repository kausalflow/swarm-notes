title: Integrated Dynamics Planning
---

**Background:** Test-time decision search for time-series control currently relies on heuristic-based wrappers rather than robustly integrated dynamics models.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to move beyond forecast-trace wrappers toward more integrated frameworks that can natively incorporate learned or adaptive dynamics models for improved planning, particularly when the coupling between actions and future feasibility sets is complex and non-myopic. Current methods struggle to maintain performance across broad, diverse control domains without specific task-level tuning.

**Why It Matters:** This reflects a foundational shift needed in time-series decision-making to move away from post-hoc wrappers toward natively dynamics-informed planners.
