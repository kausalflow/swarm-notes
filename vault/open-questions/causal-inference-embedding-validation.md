title: Causal Inference Embedding Validation
---

**Background:** High-dimensional embeddings from foundation models are increasingly used for confounding adjustment in observational causal inference. However, there is no standardized framework for integrating these latent representations into established methods like target trial emulation or instrumental variable analysis.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research must rigorously evaluate how foundation model embeddings can be systematically integrated into various causal inference frameworks. This includes validating their robustness in reducing systematic bias across diverse observational study designs where latent confounding is prevalent.

**Why It Matters:** Validating this approach is essential for moving toward automated, data-driven confounding adjustment in real-world evidence generation.
