title: Incorporating Agent Mobility Models
---

**Background:** Agent-based and network-based epidemic models often assume that individuals remain stationary, which can conflict with the high mobility observed in real-world human populations.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should address the impact of agent mobility within graph-based epidemic models to capture more realistic contact patterns and transmission dynamics. This involves moving beyond the current assumption of fixed node positions and integrating dynamic spatial movement into the particle filter framework.

**Why It Matters:** Static models often fail to capture the speed of propagation and the spatial spread of epidemics, especially during sudden surges, which limits the predictive accuracy of the particle filter for real-world scenarios.
