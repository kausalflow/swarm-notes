title: Longitudinal Impact of Proactive Scaffolding
---

**Background:** Proactive, forecast-driven instructional systems are currently evaluated primarily on immediate task performance in controlled settings, leaving their long-term pedagogical efficacy unverified.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should evaluate the longitudinal effects of proactive collaborative scaffolding, specifically focusing on durable learning outcomes, conceptual retention, and the development of self-regulatory abilities to determine if these systems foster sustainable skill acquisition.

**Why It Matters:** Distinguishing between transient performance boosts and sustainable long-term learning is critical for the evolution of pedagogical AI.
