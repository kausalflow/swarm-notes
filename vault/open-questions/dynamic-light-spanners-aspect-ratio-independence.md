title: Aspect Ratio Independent Light Spanners
---

**Background:** The efficiency of dynamic spanners in doubling metrics is often analyzed in terms of the aspect ratio ($\Phi$), which is the ratio between the maximum and minimum pairwise distances in the point set.

**Question / Future Work:** Can the runtime bound for maintaining a dynamic light spanner in doubling metrics be improved to depend only on the number of points ($n=|X|$) rather than the aspect ratio ($\Phi$)?
