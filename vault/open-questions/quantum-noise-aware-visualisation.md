title: Quantum-noise-aware RGB normalization
---

**Background:** Magnetometer triads in quantum sensing environments require distinguishing between fundamental quantum noise limits and technical sensor artefacts.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing a method to incorporate the known quantum noise floor directly into the normalization stage of RGB spectrogram generation to effectively differentiate between quantum-limited and technically limited sensor performance.

**Why It Matters:** Technically important for precise diagnostic characterization of quantum magnetometers, as it allows for an immediate visual separation of inherent quantum noise from extraneous technical faults.

**Evidence:** For quantum sensor arrays, an extension that incorporates the known quantum noise floor into the normalisation stage... could sharpen the distinction between quantum-limited and technically limited operation.
