title: Cross-site generalization challenge
---

**Background:** Predictive models for localized environmental risks are frequently developed and evaluated within specific local environments, creating significant barriers to scalable deployment.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to quantify and mitigate the degradation of model performance when applying event-based disease risk forecasting across diverse geographic regions, agricultural practices, and sensor configurations.

**Why It Matters:** Local overfitting is a primary bottleneck in precision agriculture and environmental monitoring, where models often fail to generalize to new fields or shifts in climate conditions.
