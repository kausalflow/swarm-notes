title: Online Hyperparameter Adaptation for GPs
---

**Background:** Gaussian process models depend significantly on kernel hyperparameters, yet standard marginal likelihood optimization is non-convex and often requires costly offline retraining.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing efficient online algorithms for continuous hyperparameter adaptation in sequential Gaussian process models is essential for maintaining performance in non-stationary or time-varying environments.

**Why It Matters:** Online adaptation of hyperparameters is a fundamental requirement for deploying GP models in autonomous real-time signal processing systems.
