title: Automated Riemannian manifold selection
---

**Background:** Neural activity is intrinsically constrained to low-dimensional dynamical manifolds, yet most foundation models operate within Euclidean spaces.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the optimal Riemannian manifold geometry (e.g., curvature, topology) for specific types of neural dynamics remains an open research challenge. Systematic, data-driven methods for discovering or learning these latent manifold structures are needed to move beyond pre-defined assumptions.

**Why It Matters:** Mismatched manifold geometry can introduce significant representational bias, hindering the performance of geometric foundation models across different physiological domains.
