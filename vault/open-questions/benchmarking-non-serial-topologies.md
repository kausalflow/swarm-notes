title: Benchmarking Non-Serial Supply Chains
---

**Background:** The bullwhip effect in supply chains is influenced by both supply chain topology and ordering policies, yet no standardized framework exists for evaluating these policies across different network structures.

**Question / Future Work:** While the simulation engine currently supports serial supply chains, further research is required to evaluate how the benchmarking framework performs with non-serial (convergent and divergent) network topologies. Extending the analysis to these complex graphs is a necessary step for generalizing the findings beyond serial chains.

**Why It Matters:** Multi-echelon supply chains in industry are rarely perfectly serial; therefore, validating the framework's robustness across arbitrary DAG topologies is crucial for practical applicability.
