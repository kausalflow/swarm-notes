title: Unified Anticipation and Prediction Method
---

**Background:** Early action prediction requires classifying actions from incomplete temporal sequences, necessitating models that can reason about future states given limited visual history. Current approaches frequently lack a unified objective for simultaneously handling action anticipation and early-stage classification.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop a unified architectural framework that bridges the gap between action anticipation and early action prediction. This would investigate whether a single latent objective can effectively capture the temporal evolution of actions while remaining robust to varying observation lengths and missing modalities.

**Why It Matters:** Unifying these tasks addresses the inherent redundancy in current video understanding pipelines, potentially leading to more efficient, general-purpose temporal encoders.
