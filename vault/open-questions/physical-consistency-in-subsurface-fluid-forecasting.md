title: Physical Consistency in Subsurface Forecasting
---

**Background:** Ocean forecasting models are often constrained by the need to model complex 3D structures like stratification and heat content under atmospheric forcing.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust AI architectures that maintain physical consistency and prevent over-smoothing of subsurface features like eddy kinetic energy in high-resolution, long-horizon fluid dynamics forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental challenge in bridging the performance gap between AI surrogates and traditional physical numerical models in climate science.
