title: Adaptive chaos estimation techniques

**Background:** Traffic flow forecasting models often require substantial historical data from the target city to achieve high accuracy, posing challenges for few-shot learning in data-scarce urban environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to develop adaptive chaos estimation techniques that remain robust under extreme data scarcity, where standard statistical chaos proxies may become unreliable due to insufficient historical observations.

**Why It Matters:** Improving data efficiency in chaos-informed models is essential for deploying these architectures in newly established sensor networks or cities with minimal monitoring history.

**Evidence:** Despite these advances, CIWI-CKT requires sufficient historical data for reliable chaos estimation... Future work will focus on adaptive chaos estimation for extreme data scarcity
