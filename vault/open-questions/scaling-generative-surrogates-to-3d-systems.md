title: Scaling generative surrogates to 3D
---

**Background:** Dynamical systems governed by complex partial differential equations often exhibit turbulent behavior, where autoregressive error accumulation in surrogate models causes statistical drift over long forecast horizons.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending one-step generative autoregressive frameworks to fully three-dimensional dynamical systems poses significant challenges, particularly regarding the memory and computational demands of scaling the transition kernel while preserving real-time rollout efficiency.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling to 3D is a critical bottleneck for surrogate modeling to transition from 2D slices to reliable full-volume physical simulations.
