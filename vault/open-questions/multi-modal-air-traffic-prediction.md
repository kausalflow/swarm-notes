title: Multi-modal Air Traffic Prediction
---

**Background:** Air traffic flow is significantly influenced by environmental and operational context, yet most trajectory-based models currently focus on internal dynamics rather than external drivers.

**Question / Future Work:** Integrating exogenous, multi-modal drivers like adverse weather and verbal ATC instructions into state-to-flow models is required to handle high-complexity, non-nominal traffic scenarios.

**Why It Matters:** Improving predictive robustness in volatile operational conditions requires accounting for external environmental and control variables beyond pure trajectory data.

**Evidence:** Future work may further incorporate external factors, including weather conditions and richer spoken instructions, to further enhance predictive performance under rapidly evolving traffic situations.
