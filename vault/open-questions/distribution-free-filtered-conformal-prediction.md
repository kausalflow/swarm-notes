title: Distribution-Free Filtered Conformal Prediction
---

**Background:** Filtered conformal prediction often relies on assumptions about filter stability and data-generating processes to handle serial dependence in calibration scores.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing distribution-free, model-agnostic filtered conformal prediction methods that provide rigorous finite-sample coverage guarantees without requiring stability or oracle-realizability assumptions remains a fundamental challenge for deep learning-based forecasting.

**Why It Matters:** Reliable uncertainty quantification in safety-critical domains requires moving beyond conditional validity proofs to robust, distribution-free guarantees.
