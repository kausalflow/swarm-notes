title: Theoretical stabilization regimes
---

**Background:** When learning-based control models are misspecified (e.g., due to partial observability), the resulting control performance might be stabilizing or lead to infinite costs. The authors numerically illustrated regimes where the lower-bias multi-step predictor induces a stabilizing controller while single-step methods fail.

**Question / Future Work:** A theoretical characterization of the regimes where the lower-bias multi-step predictor yields a stabilizing controller, while alternative predictors fail in the misspecified closed-loop setting, is left for future work.
