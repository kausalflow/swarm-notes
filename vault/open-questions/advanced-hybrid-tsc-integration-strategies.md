title: Advanced Hybrid TSC Integration Strategies
---

**Background:** Hybrid time series classification models often combine heterogeneous feature spaces from different methods to improve predictive performance, but simple concatenation-based integration strategies frequently fail to achieve consistent performance gains across diverse datasets.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate sophisticated strategies for integrating the feature spaces of different time series classification methods (e.g., Hydra and Quant) beyond simple concatenation, to improve predictive quality and better realize the potential of hybrid models.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for improving the efficacy of hybrid TSC models, as current straightforward concatenation methods do not reliably outperform individual baseline approaches.
