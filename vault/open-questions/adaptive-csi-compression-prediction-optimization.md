title: Adaptive CSI compression and prediction
---

**Background:** Cell-Free Massive MIMO systems rely on frequent Channel State Information (CSI) exchanges, which significantly strain transport network bandwidth in O-RAN architectures.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop mechanisms that jointly optimize CSI compression ratios and predictor architectures to enable adaptive X-haul utilization based on varying traffic demands and user mobility patterns.

**Why It Matters:** Dynamic adaptation of compression and prediction is crucial for maintaining performance and resource efficiency under unpredictable network loads in real-world O-RAN deployments.
