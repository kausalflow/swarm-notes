**Background:** Developing robust probabilistic forecasting systems benefits from combining the predictive power of different modeling paradigms.

**Question / Future Work:** Explore ensemble strategies that leverage the strengths of distinct models, such as combining the deterministic forecasting capabilities of the LSTM model with the probabilistic uncertainty quantification provided by the Normalizing Flow (FloTS) model.
