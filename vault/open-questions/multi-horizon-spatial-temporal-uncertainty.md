title: Multi-horizon uncertainty in graphs
---

**Background:** Accurate energy system management and long-term planning require robust uncertainty quantification that accounts for compounding errors across extended temporal horizons.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending spatial-temporal conformal prediction to multi-horizon settings is a significant challenge, requiring new approaches to model the compounding uncertainty and evolving correlations present in graph-structured time series over long sequences.

**Why It Matters:** Multi-horizon uncertainty quantification is a critical bottleneck for operational decisions in renewable-heavy energy grids, such as storage management and long-term generation scheduling.
