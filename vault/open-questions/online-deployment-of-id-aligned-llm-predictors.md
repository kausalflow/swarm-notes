title: Online Deployment of ID-Aligned LLM Predictors
---

**Background:** Large language models are increasingly used in production systems to generate ancillary signals for candidate generation and ranking, yet bridging the gap between LLM semantic representations and sparse, ID-centric industrial features remains a challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** The deployment and validation of Semantic ID (SID) or other ID-alignment mechanisms for LLM-based ancillary predictors under the strict latency and stability constraints of live, production-scale industrial advertising systems remain an open challenge.

**Why It Matters:** This question addresses the fundamental deployment bottleneck of aligning LLM-based predictive signals with production-scale sparse ID structures, which is critical for real-world adoption.

**Evidence:** An important direction for future work is the multimodal semantic-ID extension discussed in the project notes; however, that version has not yet been validated online and is therefore left outside the main empirical claims of this paper.
