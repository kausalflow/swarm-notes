title: Robust Information Criteria for Dynamic Estimation
---

**Background:** Robust estimation in time series is typically formulated under the assumption that contamination is local, but dynamic models cause contamination to propagate through the residual filter.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing information criteria specifically tailored for subsample-based estimators in the presence of dynamic contamination remains an open research problem, as current criteria are often biased or lack theoretical foundations.

**Why It Matters:** Model selection is critical for reliable inference in dynamic models, and existing criteria are known to be biased or inconsistent under contamination.

**Evidence:** The development of subsample-based information criteria tailored to this framework remains an open problem.
