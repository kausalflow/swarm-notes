title: Multivariate time series anomaly detection extension
---

**Background:** Univariate time series anomaly detection frequently faces challenges when extending existing methodologies to multivariate settings. Current techniques are often specialized for individual signal streams, limiting their direct application in complex, multi-sensor industrial environments.

**Question / Future Work:** The extension of wavelet-based statistical anomaly detection mechanisms to account for inter-variable dependencies in multivariate systems remains an open challenge. Adapting the current mathematical framework and hierarchical tree structure to directly process multivariate streams is necessary to handle inter-variable correlations effectively.

**Why It Matters:** Multivariate anomaly detection is critical for real-world applications involving complex systems, yet many high-performance algorithms remain limited to univariate data. Developing such an extension would significantly enhance the utility of the proposed methodology in industrial settings where sensors are highly correlated.
