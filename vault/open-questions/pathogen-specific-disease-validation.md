title: Pathogen-specific disease validation
---

**Background:** Vineyard disease forecasting typically relies on management-oriented environmental annotations that serve as proxies for, rather than direct pathological measurements of, disease severity.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing how event-based forecasting models can be validated against direct pathogen-specific measurements rather than relying on treatment-oriented proxies or environmental heuristics remains a critical challenge for biological accuracy.

**Why It Matters:** Current reliance on proxy labels may conflate management behavior with actual disease progression, limiting the scientific and practical utility of predictive models in agricultural monitoring.
