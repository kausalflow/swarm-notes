title: Large-scale Spatio-Temporal Pre-training
---

**Background:** Large-scale pre-training has shown transformative potential across diverse data modalities, including static images and natural language. The application of such paradigms to multivariate time series data remains a significant research frontier.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop large-scale spatio-temporal pre-training frameworks specifically optimized for multivariate time series, addressing challenges in data heterogeneity, temporal dependencies, and the scalability of representation learning.

**Why It Matters:** Shifting toward foundational, pre-trained models is a key bottleneck for generalizing time-series analysis across heterogeneous domains and tasks, moving beyond architecture-specific improvements.
