title: Cross-Domain Generalization of Mixing
---

**Background:** Long-term time series forecasting often involves datasets from diverse domains, such as traffic, weather, and energy, each with unique temporal characteristics and structural patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Evaluation of multi-scale temporal mixing approaches needs to be extended to a broader range of domains beyond electricity-related benchmarks to confirm the architectural generality and robustness of these methods.

**Why It Matters:** Ensuring that architectural innovations generalize across different temporal dynamics is essential for establishing them as effective universal components in forecasting systems.
