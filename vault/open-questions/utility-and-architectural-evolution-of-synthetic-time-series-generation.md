title: Synthetic data utility and evolution
---

**Background:** The generation of synthetic time series data involves complex stochastic processes often exhibiting non-stationarity, which necessitates models that move beyond traditional assumptions of weak stationarity.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to evaluate the utility of synthetic time series data in enhancing the performance of downstream decision-making tasks (e.g., trading strategies) and to explore the replacement of recurrent blocks with more advanced temporal architectures like Transformers to improve modeling fidelity.

**Why It Matters:** This highlights critical gaps in practical downstream application and architectural evolution for generative time-series models.
