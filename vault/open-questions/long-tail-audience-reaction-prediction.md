title: Addressing long-tail reaction distribution
---

**Background:** Audience reaction forecasting is often limited by the heavy-tailed distribution of emotional responses, where rare categories are difficult for current models to predict accurately.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating methods for robust reaction prediction across rare emotional categories is essential to improve the comprehensive utility of affective computing models in real-world environments.

**Why It Matters:** Effective affective computing must move beyond dominant response prediction to capture the full spectrum of collective audience reactions.

**Evidence:** Video2Reaction exhibits a long-tail distribution that remains underexplored.
