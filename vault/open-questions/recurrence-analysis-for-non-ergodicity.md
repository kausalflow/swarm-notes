title: Recurrence analysis for non-ergodicity
---

**Background:** Many-body localized systems and models exhibiting quantum many-body scars show signatures of non-ergodicity and periodic dynamics in their out-of-equilibrium evolution. Recurrence analysis serves as a framework for characterizing non-equilibrium quantum dynamics through the structural properties of recurrence plots and quantification descriptors.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains to be explored whether recurrence plot (RP) analysis and recurrence quantification analysis (RQA) can effectively diagnose ergodicity-breaking phases such as many-body localization or identify signatures of quantum many-body scarring across various experimental or simulated non-equilibrium quantum platforms. Future work should investigate whether RPs can extract critical parameters, such as the critical disorder strength for ergodicity breaking, or detect the distinct oscillatory signatures of scarred states.

**Why It Matters:** This is technically significant because RQA provides a potential unsupervised method to distinguish complex phases of matter that are currently difficult to characterize in the time domain.
