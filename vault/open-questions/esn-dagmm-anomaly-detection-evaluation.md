title: Anomaly detection evaluation for ESN-DAGMM
---

**Background:** Deep Autoencoding Gaussian Mixture Models (DAGMM) historically lack mechanisms for temporal dependencies, which are essential for processing streaming time-series data.

**Question / Future Work:** Empirical validation is needed to determine the performance of ESN-DAGMM specifically for unsupervised anomaly detection in high-volume, dynamic network telemetry environments.

**Why It Matters:** The framework's primary utility in network management depends on its capacity to robustly distinguish anomalies from high-volume telemetry patterns.

**Evidence:** While not evaluated here for anomaly detection (AD), the framework provides a flexible foundation that can support such tasks.
