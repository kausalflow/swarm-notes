title: Extend Adaptation to New Environments
---

**Background:** Generalization of predictive models to new geographical areas or changing data collection environments is a necessary step for widespread applicability.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should explore the applicability of lightweight adaptation strategies, such as online residual correction, across heterogeneous datasets, different cities, and varying sensing conditions to assess their broader utility beyond the studied environment.
