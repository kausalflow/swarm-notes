title: Selection of Risk Functionals
---

**Background:** The modeling of spatio-temporal extremes often relies on limit theorems for point processes of exceedances, which depend on the choice of site-dependent risk functionals and threshold definitions.

**Question / Future Work:** The selection of appropriate site-dependent risk functionals significantly influences the limit distribution of spatio-temporal point processes. It remains an open challenge to establish criteria for choosing these functionals in practical applications and to determine the sensitivity of inference results to these choices under varying spatial dependency structures.

**Why It Matters:** This is crucial because the choice of risk functional defines what is considered an extreme event in a spatio-temporal context, directly impacting statistical inference, risk assessment, and return level estimation.
