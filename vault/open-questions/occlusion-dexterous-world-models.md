title: Resolving Occlusion in Dexterous Modeling
---

**Background:** High-DoF dexterous manipulation involves complex object interactions where self-occlusion and intricate hand poses create persistent visual ambiguity for world models.

**Question / Future Work:** Resolving occlusion ambiguities and maintaining physically consistent hand-object interaction geometries remains a bottleneck for dexterous prediction; future research must enable inference of hidden states from limited egocentric views to prevent distortion during long-horizon interaction.

**Why It Matters:** Occlusion handling is a fundamental requirement for generalizing world models to real-world robotic tasks beyond controlled laboratory datasets.
