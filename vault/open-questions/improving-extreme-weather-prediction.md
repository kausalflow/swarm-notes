title: Extreme Weather Prediction Robustness
---

**Background:** Deep learning models for weather prediction frequently face challenges in maintaining accuracy and reliability when confronted with extreme weather phenomena, such as intense heat waves or severe storms, which diverge from typical climatological patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should focus on enhancing the sensitivity and robustness of deep learning architectures to extreme weather events, ensuring that forecasting systems can maintain performance and provide accurate, reliable predictions even during rare, high-impact meteorological occurrences.

**Why It Matters:** Extreme weather events have significant societal and economic impacts; improving model performance during these conditions is critical for emergency preparedness and public safety.
