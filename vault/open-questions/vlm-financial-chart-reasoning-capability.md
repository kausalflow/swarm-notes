title: Evaluating VLM Financial Chart Reasoning
---

**Background:** Vision-language models are increasingly applied to stock price forecasting using visual representations of market data, yet their internal comprehension of specific technical chart structures remains largely unverified.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work must distinguish between models that perform visual pattern matching and those that genuinely understand financial technical concepts like resistance levels, support levels, and specific chart patterns. This requires moving beyond forecasting performance toward semantic evaluation through datasets with expert-level annotations.

**Why It Matters:** Distinguishing between surface-level pattern matching and conceptual understanding is necessary to build trustworthy financial decision-support systems.
