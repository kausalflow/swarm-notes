title: Multi-Agent Prompt-Level Sharing
---

**Background:** Cooperative perception in multi-agent autonomous driving systems frequently encounters time-varying communication constraints, such as fluctuations in signal quality and bandwidth.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to formalize how multi-agent systems can share perception features at the semantic/prompt level to ensure robust collaboration despite dynamic, unreliable communication channels.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling collaborative intelligence while accounting for realistic communication bottlenecks is a critical challenge for urban-scale autonomous deployment.
