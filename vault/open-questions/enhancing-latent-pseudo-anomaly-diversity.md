title: Enhancing latent pseudo-anomaly diversity
---

**Background:** Time-series anomaly detection models frequently rely on latent space generative processes to produce pseudo-anomalies for training, yet the diversity of these generated samples is often restricted.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should focus on enhancing the latent-space anomaly perturbation process by enforcing greater diversity among generated pseudo-anomalies, such as through advanced latent sampling or explicit manifold constraints.

**Why It Matters:** Limited pseudo-anomaly diversity restricts model robustness and prevents generalization to the highly heterogeneous anomalies found in real-world time-series datasets.
