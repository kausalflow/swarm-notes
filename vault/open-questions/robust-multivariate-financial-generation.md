title: Robust multivariate financial generation
---

**Background:** Financial institutions increasingly rely on synthetic time-series for risk assessment and market simulation, yet maintaining realistic inter-asset correlations over long horizons remains challenging.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need for robust, scalable methodologies that preserve complex, evolving cross-asset dependencies across arbitrarily long sequences, particularly under non-stationary market conditions and during systemic liquidity crises where correlation structures undergo significant, rapid shifts.

**Why It Matters:** Correlation dynamics are fundamental to portfolio risk management; failing to accurately model them limits the utility of synthetic data for counterfactual analysis and stress testing.

**Evidence:** none of these methods provides a scalable solution for generating multi-stock series that capture evolving interactions and dependencies among different assets.
