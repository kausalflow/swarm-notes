title: Multivariate time series extension
---

**Background:** Most real-world time series datasets in IoT, finance, and healthcare contain multiple interacting variables, while many deep clustering architectures remain optimized for univariate sequences.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending Mamba-based clustering architectures to effectively capture inter-variable dependencies in multivariate time series remains an important open challenge for domain applicability.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical architectural bottleneck for scaling unsupervised representation learning from univariate experimental benchmarks to complex multivariate industrial data.
