title: Disentangling Operational States
---

**Background:** Wind power forecasting models often struggle with the entanglement of physically available power potential and latent, time-varying operational states such as maintenance or curtailment.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research needs to address how to better isolate latent operational interventions from meteorological fluctuations, particularly in cases where meteorological inputs are noisy or incomplete.

**Why It Matters:** This highlights a fundamental bottleneck in the effective separation of exogenous environmental signals from endogenous operational state changes in industrial time-series.

**Evidence:** Future work will explore the integration of additional meteorological observations to better capture local weather dynamics and further improve forecasting reliability.
