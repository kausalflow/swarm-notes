title: Improving High-Frequency Fidelity
---

**Background:** Spatiotemporal predictive learning models aim to forecast future frames from historical observations, often struggling with capturing long-range dependencies while preserving high-frequency details like textures and boundaries.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should explore how to further mitigate the smoothing bias observed in all models, including WaveSFNet, to more accurately capture high-frequency components in spatiotemporal predictions, as spectral analysis shows deviation from ground truth in these regions.
