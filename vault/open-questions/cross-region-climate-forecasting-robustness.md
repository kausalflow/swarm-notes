title: Cross-Region Climate Forecasting Robustness
---

**Background:** Carbon emission forecasting often faces severe performance degradation during cross-region or cross-city transfer due to differences in local infrastructure and climate conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate robust model architectures and domain adaptation techniques to overcome the performance gap between in-distribution and out-of-distribution (OOD) carbon emission data, moving beyond current sector-factor lookup methods.

**Why It Matters:** The paper identifies the in-distribution to out-of-distribution transfer gap as the dominant bottleneck in climate forecasting, making this the primary research priority.
