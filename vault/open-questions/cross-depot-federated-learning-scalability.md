title: Cross-depot FL Scalability
---

**Background:** Federated learning enables collaborative model training across decentralized datasets, but scaling these predictive systems from intra-depot station-level partitions to cross-depot architectures remains a significant challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Scaling federated learning for EV charging demand from intra-depot settings to cross-depot architectures introduces increased data heterogeneity, varying network conditions, and communication complexity that requires further research for scalable deployment.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing cross-depot scalability is essential for real-world adoption, as charging networks are geographically distributed rather than localized to a single depot.
