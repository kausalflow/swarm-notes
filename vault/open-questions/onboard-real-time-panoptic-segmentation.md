title: Onboard Real-Time Panoptic Segmentation
---

**Background:** Panoptic segmentation integrates semantic and instance segmentation into a unified framework to classify both 'stuff' (e.g., ground) and 'things' (e.g., individual trees).

**Question / Future Work:** Most current high-fidelity panoptic segmentation models for forest environments rely on computationally intensive offline processing of aggregated LiDAR scans. Extending these capabilities to real-time, single-scan inference on-board resource-constrained robotic hardware remains an open challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Real-time on-board semantic scene understanding is vital for autonomous robotic navigation and decision-making in highly dynamic, unstructured environments like forests.
