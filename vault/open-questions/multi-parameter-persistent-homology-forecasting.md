title: Multi-parameter persistent homology filtrations
---

**Background:** Topological data analysis typically relies on single-parameter filtrations based on a fixed distance metric to summarize the geometric structure of a dataset.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop multi-parameter persistent homology methods that filter data along multiple dimensions simultaneously, such as scale and demand volatility, to capture geometric properties missed by single-parameter summaries.

**Why It Matters:** Multi-parameter persistent homology is a significant advancement in TDA that could offer more nuanced geometric descriptions of complex, multi-modal data manifolds.

**Evidence:** multi-parameter persistent homology filtrating along scale and demand volatility jointly, to capture geometry that single-parameter summaries miss.
