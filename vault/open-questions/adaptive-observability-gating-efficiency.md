title: Adaptive Observability Gating Efficiency
---

**Background:** Time series forecasting models often rely on an oracle assumption where the existence and timestamps of future observations are known in advance. However, in many real-world systems, future observability itself is stochastic and dependent on complex system dynamics.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop adaptive architectures that dynamically adjust computational resources based on detected data sparsity, effectively bypassing observation-aware modules when missingness is negligible to improve real-time efficiency.

**Why It Matters:** Improving computational efficiency is crucial for deploying time series forecasting models in real-time, large-scale industrial settings.
