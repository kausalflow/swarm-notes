title: Validation on Measured Sensor Data
---

**Background:** Floating offshore structures undergo complex hydrodynamic interactions that can be described by time-domain Cummins equations involving unknown hydrodynamic coefficients.

**Question / Future Work:** Validation against real-world measured time-series data, which contains sensor noise and modeling uncertainties, is required to determine the practical applicability of using PINNs to estimate hydrodynamic coefficients for digital twin systems.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for transitioning from synthetic verification to real-world engineering and structural monitoring deployments.

**Evidence:** A current limitation of the work is that the proposed idea is based on time-series data generated by numerical simulations to verify the concept... In ongoing work, we will explore the idea in the context of measured time-series data from sensors that may also exhibit noise.
