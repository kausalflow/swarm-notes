title: Robust Virality Definition Metrics
---

**Background:** Virality in social media is frequently defined using arbitrary engagement thresholds or static volume-based metrics that fail to account for community context or temporal dynamics.

**Question / Future Work:** Alternative, more robust definitions of virality based on survival analysis, hazard rates, or multi-level engagement bands need to be developed to better quantify sustained attention and temporal engagement patterns across different social media environments.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing a consistent, robust definition of virality is essential for improving the comparability of predictive models across different datasets and research studies.
