title: SDC Interoperability and Standardization
---

**Background:** The deployment of multi-tenant Space Data Centers (SDCs) requires standardized protocols for resource provisioning, service orchestration, and data exchange between heterogeneous satellite platforms.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing universally accepted open standards and APIs is necessary to enable interoperability between diverse satellite hardware, ground infrastructure, and third-party software services, preventing vendor lock-in and supporting the growth of an integrated space-based cloud ecosystem.

**Why It Matters:** Standardization is essential for transitioning from proprietary, mission-specific designs to a commoditized and scalable space-as-a-service economy.
