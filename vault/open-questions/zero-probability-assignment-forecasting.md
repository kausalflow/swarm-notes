title: Impact of Zero Probability Assignments
---

**Background:** Human judgment forecasting platforms often restrict users to non-zero probability assignments for event outcomes.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to evaluate how the restriction of assigning zero probability to impossible outcomes affects the calibration and reliability of crowdsourced human judgment ensembles. Comparing performance between platforms that allow versus forbid zero-probability assignment could reveal significant improvements in forecast accuracy.

**Why It Matters:** This is a critical, actionable improvement for public health forecasting platforms to increase the reliability of crowd-sourced probability assessments.
