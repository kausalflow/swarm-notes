title: Integrating Magnetospheric State Variables
---

**Background:** Auroral electrojet (AE) index forecasting typically relies on upstream solar wind observations, which limits predictability due to the neglect of internal magnetospheric processes such as reconnection and mass loading.

**Question / Future Work:** The potential for integrating magnetospheric preconditioning and internal state variables alongside solar wind turbulence diagnostics remains an open avenue for improving long-range geomagnetic index forecasting, particularly for moving beyond solar-wind-driven models toward a holistic representation of the Sun-Earth system.

**Why It Matters:** This direction addresses the fundamental upper bound of solar wind-driven predictability by proposing a more holistic representation of magnetospheric states.

**Evidence:** Incorporating proxies for magnetospheric preconditioning may further improve forecasts, particularly at longer horizons. ... Other future work could explore ... integrate turbulence diagnostics with state-space models.
