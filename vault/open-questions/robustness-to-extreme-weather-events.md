title: Extreme Weather Robustness
---

**Background:** Attention-based models often rely on patterns present in training data, which can limit their performance when encountering extreme weather events or unprecedented environmental conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is needed to improve the robustness and generalizability of attention-based models to extreme weather events and rare environmental anomalies. This involves evaluating models on diverse, climatically distinct datasets and exploring architectural modifications to handle extrapolation better than standard attention mechanisms.

**Why It Matters:** Reliable forecasting during extreme events is critical for grid stability and the prevention of system-wide failures.
