title: Cross-Indication Parameter Transportability
---

**Background:** Cross-indication hierarchical pooling can be evaluated to determine whether joint kinetic parameter estimation improves early clinical trial forecasts across diverse cancer types.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate whether cross-indication hierarchical pooling—fitting joint mechanistic tumour dynamics and survival models across trials from different tumour types—can effectively improve out-of-sample forecasts for new indications with limited historical data.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the limits of parameter transportability across different oncology indications is crucial for expanding the applicability of mechanistic joint models to rare or novel cancer types where historical trial data is extremely sparse.
