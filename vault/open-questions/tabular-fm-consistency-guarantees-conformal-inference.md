title: Theoretical consistency of tabular FMs
---

**Background:** Tabular foundation models, such as those trained on synthetic priors, perform in-context learning for various predictive tasks without requiring fine-tuning, yet they lack established theoretical guarantees for consistent estimation of conditional distributions.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to establish a theoretical framework or formal consistency guarantees for applying tabular foundation models to conditional quantile estimation within conformal inference tasks, particularly to ensure valid coverage in safety-critical applications.

**Why It Matters:** Rigorous consistency guarantees are necessary for the widespread adoption of foundation models in safety-critical infrastructures where probabilistic coverage must be statistically sound.
