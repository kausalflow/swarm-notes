title: Improve latent compression resolution
---

**Background:** The accuracy of deep learning weather models is fundamentally limited by the reconstruction error inherent in the autoencoder used to compress atmospheric fields into a latent space.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should explore higher-resolution latent compressions, as the current spatial resolution (1.5°) constrains the model's ability to capture fine-scale atmospheric features, such as the intensity of tropical cyclones.
