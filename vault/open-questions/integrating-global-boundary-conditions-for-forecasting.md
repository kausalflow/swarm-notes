title: Integrating global boundary conditions
---

**Background:** Tropical cyclone track forecasting involves predicting complex, large-scale atmospheric movements over extended durations, yet deep learning models often struggle with performance degradation at longer lead times.

**Question / Future Work:** Integrating global boundary conditions from planetary-scale models into localized, high-resolution deep learning frameworks remains an unresolved challenge. Bridging this gap is necessary to improve long-term track forecasting accuracy by effectively capturing macroscopic atmospheric adjustments like the movement of the subtropical high.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing the spatial scale limitation is critical for advancing the reliability of data-driven models in long-range tropical cyclone track forecasting where large-scale steering flows dominate.
