title: Real-time scalability of MS-SVDD
---

**Background:** Subspace Support Vector Data Description (SVDD) methods project multimodal data into a shared low-dimensional subspace for one-class classification. While graph-embedded regularization improves subspace learning, its feasibility in large-scale or highly dynamic systems is limited by computational and operational constraints.

**Question / Future Work:** Assessing the performance of graph-regularized MS-SVDD in environments with live operational constraints such as communication latency, high-volume data streaming, and dynamic topology shifts. Determining the optimal trade-off between the increased robustness of graph regularization and the resulting computational overhead for real-time critical infrastructure monitoring.

**Why It Matters:** Addresses the critical gap between proposed algorithmic robustness and practical, real-world deployment requirements for infrastructure-critical monitoring systems.
