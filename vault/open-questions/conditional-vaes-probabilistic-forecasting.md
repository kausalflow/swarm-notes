**Background:** Probabilistic forecasting allows for the application of uncertainty quantification to inform operational decision-making processes and risk assessment.

**Question / Future Work:** Consider other generative architectures for probabilistic forecasting, such as conditional variational autoencoders (VAEs), which learn a conditional latent representation of the future given past observations.
