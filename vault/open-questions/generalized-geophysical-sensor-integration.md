title: Generalized Geophysical Sensor Integration
---

**Background:** Foundation models for Earth observation often focus on specific modalities. Broadening the scope to include diverse, non-atmospheric sensor types is key for generalized Earth system simulators.

**Question / Future Work:** The latent observation space needs to be expanded to integrate a wider array of geophysical instruments and modalities beyond atmosphere/ocean data, such as seismic or sub-surface waves, to enable generalized Earth system simulation.

**Why It Matters:** A comprehensive Earth digital twin requires integrating heterogeneous geophysical data across multiple spheres to fully capture complex Earth system interactions.

**Evidence:** Second, expanding the latent observation space to integrate more instruments and modalities—such as phased arrays and seismic waves—would generalize the latent Earth observation space.
