title: Cross-Environment Channel Generalization
---

**Background:** Data-driven channel prediction models for vehicular communications often rely on limited datasets, which can restrict their ability to generalize across diverse, non-stationary propagation environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to investigate whether generative channel models can generalize effectively across different urban topographies, mobility patterns, and propagation environments, rather than overfitting to a single measurement campaign.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization is a critical bottleneck for data-driven channel models. Without evaluating on broader datasets, it is difficult to determine if high performance in a single campaign translates to the high-reliability requirements of global 6G vehicular connectivity.

**Evidence:** evaluating the proposed framework across multiple routes and propagation environments would further assess model generalization.
