title: Industrial Multimodal Diagnostic Reasoning
---

**Background:** Industrial Prognostics and Health Management (PHM) models currently focus primarily on numerical forecasting and classification tasks, often lacking mechanisms for advanced diagnostic reasoning or semantic interpretability.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop methods for industrial foundation models to perform time-series-to-text question answering, enabling systems to interpret sensor sequences and provide natural-language maintenance-oriented diagnostics.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between numerical predictive outputs and explainable, maintenance-focused reasoning is critical for the real-world deployment of foundation models in industrial diagnostics.

**Evidence:** The current framework mainly focuses on numerical prediction and classification, while natural-language diagnostic reasoning is still underexplored.
