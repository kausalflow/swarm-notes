title: Cross-Platform Popularity Generalization
---

**Background:** Short-form video popularity is highly dependent on platform-specific exposure biases and recommendation dynamics that vary across service providers.

**Question / Future Work:** Evaluating the transferability and cross-platform generalizability of popularity forecasting models remains a major challenge. Further research is required to understand how platform-specific exposure biases and recommendation mechanics interact with open-web context across diverse social ecosystems.

**Why It Matters:** Critical for determining whether open-web grounding provides a universal signal or is fundamentally limited by platform-specific closed-loop dynamics.

**Evidence:** the evidence-to-popularity mapping learned on YouTube Shorts may not transfer directly to platforms such as TikTok, Kuaishou, or Instagram Reels.
