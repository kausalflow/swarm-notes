title: Autonomous Evolution of Governance Strategies
---

**Background:** Verbal reinforcement learning currently relies on manually designed logic for knowledge curation and governance.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate if agents can autonomously evolve their own curation, conflict resolution, and deprecation strategies based on world feedback, shifting from optimizing knowledge content to optimizing the learning mechanism itself.

**Why It Matters:** Moving from fixed governance logic to autonomous self-improving curation is a significant milestone for building long-lived, adaptive LLM agents.

**Evidence:** Can world feedback also drive the evolution of the curation mechanism itself? ... Applying this to the curation loop would close a second feedback loop: world outcomes improving not just what the agent knows, but how the agent manages what it knows.
