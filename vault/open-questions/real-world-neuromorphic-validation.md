title: Real-world Neuromorphic System Validation
---

**Background:** Spiking neural networks (SNNs) offer energy-efficient inference on neuromorphic hardware, but optimizing their training and deployment for diverse, real-world signal processing tasks remains challenging.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research needs to focus on advancing end-to-end training methodologies and conducting real-world on-chip validation to improve the robustness and practical utility of neuromorphic frameworks for diverse application tasks.

**Why It Matters:** Transitioning from simulation or specific dataset evaluation to robust, real-world deployment is critical for the practical adoption of neuromorphic computing in field-deployed devices.

**Evidence:** In our future work, we plan to delve into end-to-end training and real-world on-chip validation to bolster the framework’s robustness and practicality on more application tasks.
