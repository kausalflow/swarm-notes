title: Adaptive Multimodal Fusion Resilience
---

**Background:** Deep learning models for photovoltaic (PV) power generation forecasting often struggle to maintain accuracy when weather conditions deviate significantly from expected or historical patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved need to develop adaptive joint fusion layers capable of dynamically adjusting the influence of different input modalities (such as PV production, weather history, and future forecasts) in response to changing meteorological conditions, ensuring robustness when patterns shift unexpectedly.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a primary bottleneck in current multimodal forecasting architectures, where fixed fusion strategies fail to handle non-stationary or adverse environmental scenarios.
