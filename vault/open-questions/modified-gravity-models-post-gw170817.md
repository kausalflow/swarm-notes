**Background:** Investigating deviations from General Relativity (GR) using galaxy cluster gravitational redshifts is a key goal for future cosmological surveys.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should investigate how modified gravity models that remain viable after the GW170817 event alter the gravitational potentials of galaxy clusters.
