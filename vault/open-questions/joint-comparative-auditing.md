title: Joint and Comparative Auditing
---

**Background:** Sequential auditing frameworks evaluate conditional quantile forecasters primarily on individual quantile levels. There is no standard mechanism for evaluating cross-quantile consistency or performing head-to-head model comparisons within this framework.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop extensions to the sequential betting machinery that allow for the aggregation of miscalibration evidence across multiple quantile levels simultaneously, or facilitate comparative 'head-to-head' auditing between two different forecasting models.

**Why It Matters:** Aggregating evidence across quantiles and comparative evaluation are critical for holistic model monitoring and decision-making in real-world deployments.
