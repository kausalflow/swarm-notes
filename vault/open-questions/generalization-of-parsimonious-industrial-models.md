title: Generalization of Parsimonious Models
---

**Background:** Industrial systems often follow underlying physical laws that are inherently parsimonious, yet data-driven modeling approaches like deep neural networks often lack this structural constraint. Existing methods for identifying explicit piece-wise polynomial relationships may fail to generalize effectively when trained on incomplete datasets typical of industrial environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is needed to improve the generalization of explicit piece-wise polynomial models in high-dimensional settings, specifically addressing observed performance degradation on complex operational axes in robotic manipulators compared to more stable ones.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization to unseen operational contexts remains a primary bottleneck for deploying data-driven industrial models, and understanding why specific model architectures fail on certain axes is essential for reliability.
