title: Extending V-NSDE Framework Capabilities
---

**Background:** Variational Neural Stochastic Differential Equations (V-NSDEs) provide a framework for modeling complex, continuous-time dynamical systems by combining latent stochastic processes with deep learning, but their application currently relies on static representations for heterogeneous entities.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to incorporate time-varying embeddings, exogenous control variables, and hierarchical spatial dependencies into the V-NSDE framework to better capture evolving socioeconomic dynamics and inter-district relationships.

**Why It Matters:** These extensions address the limitations of the current model in handling non-stationary and non-independent socioeconomic influences, which are critical for practical policy-making and forecasting.
