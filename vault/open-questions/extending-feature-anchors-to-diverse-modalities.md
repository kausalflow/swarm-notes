title: Extending feature anchors to diverse modalities
---

**Background:** Handcrafted time-series features provide an explicit statistical scaffold, yet the methodology for treating them as feature anchors has primarily been evaluated on inertial measurement unit (IMU) data.

**Question / Future Work:** Generalizing the feature-anchor framework to other modalities like ECG or PPG requires investigating whether distinct domain-specific feature families and corresponding context-adaptation modules can maintain similar performance gains and interpretability.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing the universality of the feature-anchor design principle beyond specific sensor types is a key research bottleneck.
