title: Integrating Spatiotemporal Network Context
---

**Background:** Network performance optimization in modern cellular systems requires understanding the spatiotemporal interdependencies between geographically adjacent cells, as local network conditions are often influenced by the behavior of neighboring infrastructure.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to integrate spatio-temporal context from neighboring cells into LLM-based forecasting and diagnostic frameworks to enable holistic, closed-loop resource orchestration.

**Why It Matters:** Integrating neighboring cell context is essential for achieving truly proactive and adaptive network management in dense 5G deployments where performance issues are spatially correlated.
