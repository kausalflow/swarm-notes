title: Refining Latent Intensity and Residuals
---

**Background:** High-frequency financial duration modeling often relies on self-exciting point processes to capture market activity patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Further investigation is required to determine whether alternative nonlinear feedback mechanisms or more complex latent processes can better capture the intricate empirical distributions of interarrival times than current flexible residual specifications allow.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a fundamental limitation in current microstructure models where state-of-the-art intensity dynamics fail to fully account for the heavy-tailed nature of financial event durations.
