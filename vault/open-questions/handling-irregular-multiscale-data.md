title: Handling Irregularly Sampled Data
---

**Background:** Many forecasting architectures based on reservoir computing and state-space models assume uniformly sampled data to maintain internal state integrity. Real-world observational data is frequently sampled irregularly or at multiple scales, challenging the deployment of such models.

**Question / Future Work:** Research methods for adapting state-based forecasting models to handle irregular or multiscale temporal sampling without losing state coherence or predictive performance.

**Why It Matters:** Real-world observational time series are rarely uniform; solving for irregular sampling is a critical step for practical applicability in clinical and environmental monitoring.
