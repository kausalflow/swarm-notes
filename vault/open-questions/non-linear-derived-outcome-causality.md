title: Non-linear causal inference models
---

**Background:** Current methods for causal inference on derived multivariate outcomes are often restricted to linear or weakly stationary assumptions.

**Question / Future Work:** Extension of causal inference frameworks for derived outcomes to accommodate non-linear, non-stationary temporal dynamics while maintaining statistical guarantees.

**Why It Matters:** Necessary for moving beyond linear autoregressive models in complex dynamical systems.
