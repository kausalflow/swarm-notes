title: Zero-shot distribution shift evaluation
---

**Background:** Generative models for time-series are often evaluated on known datasets or patterns, leaving their true zero-shot capability across distribution shifts untested.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematically evaluate unified generative time-series models under true zero-shot conditions, particularly regarding their ability to synthesize patterns in unseen temporal domains or datasets without additional training.

**Why It Matters:** Verifying zero-shot transferability is essential to determine whether generative frameworks are learning reusable domain-invariant temporal structures or merely memorizing training datasets.
