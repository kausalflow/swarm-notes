title: Reasoning vs Numerical Precision Tradeoff
---

**Background:** Large language models (LLMs) used for time series forecasting often struggle to simultaneously maintain high general-purpose reasoning ability and numerical forecasting precision within a single unified framework.

**Question / Future Work:** The challenge of balancing semantic reasoning with numerical accuracy persists despite current agentic frameworks, necessitating future work on robust architectures that can maintain cross-domain generalization while executing precise, evidence-guided numerical corrections under non-stationary conditions.

**Why It Matters:** This is the core unresolved bottleneck identified by the paper, motivating the need for role-specialized designs in agentic forecasting.
