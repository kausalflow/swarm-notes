title: Distributions for General Processes
---

**Background:** Transcripts represent the composition of an ordinal pattern and the inverse of its predecessor, offering a symbolic way to measure differences between successive ordinal patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to derive analytical distributions for a wider variety of stochastic processes, as current results are limited to specific cases like i.i.d. and random walk processes. Extending these to broader classes would enhance the robustness of nonparametric dependence tests.

**Why It Matters:** Generalizing these analytical distributions would enable more reliable and versatile statistical diagnostic tools for serial dependence.

**Evidence:** Furthermore, it would be relevant to obtain closed-form distributions and asymptotics for further types of process than those considered here.
