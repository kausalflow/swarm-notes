title: Multimodal Context Integration Challenges
---

**Background:** Multimodal time-series forecasting benchmarks often suffer from limited scope, synthetic bias, and data leakage, hindering the objective evaluation of foundation models.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing robust methodologies for integrating diverse textual contexts—such as metadata, news, and external covariates—into time-series forecasting remains a key research challenge. Future work is required to determine how to better utilize such information beyond simple ensemble approaches and to differentiate between model failure due to poor context quality versus ineffective fusion architectures.

**Why It Matters:** The authors demonstrate that current models often struggle to leverage text effectively on robust benchmarks, identifying the integration of multi-domain context as a primary bottleneck for achieving generalization.
