title: Forecasting Stochastic Switching Dynamics
---

**Background:** Cardiac rhythm, sleep staging, and autonomic tone fluctuations in physiological signals are characterized by stochastic transitions between states, which deterministic forecasting models often struggle to replicate.

**Question / Future Work:** The preservation of stochastic state-transition dynamics remains an unresolved problem for deterministic point-forecasting architectures, necessitating research into latent-state or distributional forecasting mechanisms to better capture the probabilistic structure of health signals.

**Why It Matters:** Identifies a fundamental limitation of the dominant point-estimation forecasting paradigm in representing the probabilistic nature of complex physiological states.

**Evidence:** none, however, reliably preserves stochastic switching.
