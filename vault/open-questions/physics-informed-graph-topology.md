title: Physics-Informed Graph Topology
---

**Background:** Ionospheric irregularities manifest with field-aligned structures, yet most current GNSS forecasting models rely on purely geometric distance-based graph topologies.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing graph topologies that incorporate physical priors, specifically magnetic field line connectivity, is a crucial next step to better reflect the underlying plasma dynamics of the ionosphere compared to purely geometric K-nearest neighbor approaches.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond purely geometric graph constructions is essential for improving the physical realism and long-term predictive skill of GNNs in space weather, where plasma structures are inherently constrained by the Earth's magnetic field.
