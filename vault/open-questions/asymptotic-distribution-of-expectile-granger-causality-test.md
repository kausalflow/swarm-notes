title: Test Statistic Asymptotic Distribution

**Background:** The development of statistical tests for Granger causality aims to determine if past information from one time series significantly improves the prediction of another time series. Expectiles generalize mean-based measures to capture dependence across any quantile or risk level of the conditional distribution.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the asymptotic distribution of the M-vine test statistic under the null hypothesis of no Granger causality in expectiles, particularly when dependence structures are complex or when the true distribution deviates slightly from the fitted M-vine copula model assumptions.
