title: Effective Time Series Representation for MLLMs
---

**Background:** Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) offer new potential for time series analysis by processing visual representations of data. However, there is a lack of effective, standardized methods for encoding diverse time series structures as inputs for these models.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust methods to represent time series data for MLLMs is necessary to overcome current visual representation limitations, such as resolution loss, and to reduce model hallucinations in complex numerical sequences.

**Why It Matters:** The effectiveness of MLLM-based time series analysis is fundamentally bottlenecked by how temporal dynamics are translated into visual or tokenized input formats.

**Evidence:** The future work can explore a more effective way to represent time series beyond raw images and reduce MLLMs’ hallucinations.
