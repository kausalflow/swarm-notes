title: Dynamic network-temporal model weighting
---

**Background:** Traditional time-series models often rely on stationary temporal patterns that degrade significantly during global shocks or structural economic transitions.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the optimal threshold for switching between or weighting traditional temporal models and structural network-based indicators during periods of economic instability or regime change.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the critical challenge of model robustness and adaptive weighting when historical temporal dependencies fail.

**Evidence:** during the COVID-19 pandemic, when traditional forecasting accuracy collapsed ... network-enhanced models maintained substantially better performance
