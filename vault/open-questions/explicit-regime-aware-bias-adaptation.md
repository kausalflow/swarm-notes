title: Regime-Aware Bias Adaptation
---

**Background:** Robust generalization in non-stationary environments like financial markets often requires models to adaptively utilize different structural assumptions about temporal data.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work involves developing methods for explicit regime-aware bias adaptation that builds upon the emergent conditional bias utilization observed, potentially by integrating explicit regime classification or conditioning mechanisms into the distillation process.
