title: Modeling dynamic road topology
---

**Background:** Vision-language-action (VLA) models for autonomous driving typically focus on the trajectory prediction of individual agents.

**Question / Future Work:** Existing world-cognitive frameworks often neglect the dynamic evolution of road geometry and map topology, which are essential for robust scene understanding and long-term trajectory planning. Research is needed to integrate these structural environmental dynamics into VLA world models to achieve a more comprehensive representation of the driving environment.

**Why It Matters:** Expanding world cognition from agent-centric dynamics to include static environment evolution is critical for navigating complex scenarios where infrastructure constraints influence path planning.
