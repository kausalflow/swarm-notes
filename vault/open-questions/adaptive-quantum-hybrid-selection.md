title: Adaptive Hybrid Quantum Selection
---

**Background:** Hybrid quantum-classical transformer models, such as those employing quantum-enhanced value projections, have demonstrated potential for time-series forecasting, yet the benefit of quantum integration versus classical control remains ambiguous.

**Question / Future Work:** The formal classification of the quantum-machine-learning landscape is needed to determine which signal regimes truly benefit from quantum substrates. Further work is required to define adaptive hybrid strategies that selectively engage quantum layers based on detected temporal signal characteristics.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for avoiding the development of quantum architectures that perform well only due to unintentional classical bottlenecks rather than genuine quantum advantages.
