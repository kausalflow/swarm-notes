title: Scaling to Larger UAV Swarms
---

**Background:** Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) swarm control involves managing complex, nonlinear inter-agent interactions within strict real-time operational constraints. While current methods exist for small-scale formations, scaling these systems to larger swarms remains a fundamental challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to extend the existing unified framework for 4D UAV swarm trajectory prediction and distributed control to larger swarm sizes, as current validation is limited to three-UAV formations.

**Why It Matters:** Ensuring the scalability of distributed control algorithms is critical for the practical deployment of UAV swarms in diverse, large-scale industrial and urban applications.
