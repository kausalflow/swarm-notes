title: Multimodal Healthcare Foundation Models
---

**Background:** Healthcare foundation models often rely on administrative claims, which lack the granular clinical detail provided by EHRs, imaging, or genomic data. Scaling these models to integrate heterogeneous data sources remains a primary technical obstacle in clinical AI.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop multimodal healthcare foundation models that combine claims data with other clinical sources to capture both the longitudinal breadth of health records and the detailed physiological depth required for individual patient care. Future work should address technical hurdles in data harmonization and representation learning across these modalities.

**Why It Matters:** Multimodal integration is critical for transitioning foundation models from population-level pattern recognition to clinically actionable, patient-specific insights.
