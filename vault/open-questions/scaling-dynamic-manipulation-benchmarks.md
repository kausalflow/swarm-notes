title: Scaling Dynamic Manipulation Benchmarks
---

**Background:** Dynamic manipulation tasks often involve complex, non-static environments, and current robotic benchmarks primarily focus on static or quasi-static scenarios.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing large-scale, comprehensive benchmarks for dynamic object manipulation that scale to hundreds or thousands of tasks remains an unresolved challenge. Existing frameworks are limited in scope and diversity, necessitating more robust and scalable evaluation environments.

**Why It Matters:** Scalability is essential for evaluating the generalization capabilities of future embodied AI models in complex, unstructured dynamic environments.
