title: Criteria for ABM necessity
---

**Background:** Mathematical modeling of spatial biological processes often involves a choice between partial differential equation (PDE) models and agent-based models (ABMs), yet there are no definitive, objective criteria for when one modality is superior to the other.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a lack of clear guidelines or signatures in biological datasets that objectively necessitate the use of an agent-based approach over a continuous partial differential equation approach. Future work is required to define these signatures, determine the specific biological scenarios where ABM complexity is essential, and develop methods to detect these requirements a priori.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing these guidelines is crucial for modelers to select the most appropriate and parsimonious modeling modality, avoiding unnecessary computational overhead or oversimplification of biological phenomena.
