title: Stellar Collision IMF Uncertainty
---

**Background:** Massive star clusters are expected to form intermediate-mass black holes through purely stellar collision channels if their collision timescale is short enough.

**Question / Future Work:** The precise role of the initial mass function (IMF) on the black hole formation efficiency ($\epsilon_{BH}$) in purely stellar collision scenarios requires further refinement, as the current framework relies on fitting relations validated against local systems, though some uncertainty remains due to IMF variations.
