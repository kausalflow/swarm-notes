title: Visual World Models for Quadrotors
---

**Background:** World models for quadrotor control are currently constrained by reliance on low-dimensional state inputs, as extending to high-dimensional visual observations like RGB/RGB-D images introduces severe computational and representation challenges.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to extend latent dynamics world models to high-dimensional egocentric visual observations, balancing computational efficiency with the need to focus on task-relevant features rather than irrelevant visual detail.

**Why It Matters:** This is a major bottleneck for scaling aerial robotics to unstructured, real-world environments where high-fidelity state estimation (e.g., motion capture) is unavailable.

**Evidence:** Future works will extend this framework from low-dimensional state inputs to high-dimensional observations such as RGB and RGB-D images.
