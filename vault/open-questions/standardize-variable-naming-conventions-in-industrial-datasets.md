title: Standardizing Industrial Variable Naming
---

**Background:** Oil well monitoring often relies on heterogeneous sensors and localized technical nomenclature, hindering global research utility.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematizing the translation and standardization of variable names from local languages and legacy technical acronyms into English is required to enhance the dataset's global usability and cross-institutional research collaboration.

**Why It Matters:** Consistent terminology is critical for the reproducibility and widespread adoption of specialized industrial time-series datasets in the broader ML community.

**Evidence:** Note that some variable names contain terms or acronyms in Portuguese... Translating all these names into English is a pending issue that will be resolved in future versions of 3W Dataset.
