title: Multi-Objective Optimization for Deployment
---

**Background:** Autonomous ML optimization systems currently focus on optimizing a single performance metric.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should extend the framework to support multi-objective optimization, jointly optimizing for competing goals such as accuracy, inference latency, and model size to facilitate deployment-aware algorithm generation.
