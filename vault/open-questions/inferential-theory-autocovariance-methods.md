title: Inferential theory development
---

**Background:** Inferential theory for factor models in high dimensions is well-established for covariance-based methods, whereas corresponding theory for autocovariance-based methods remains largely undeveloped.

**Question / Future Work:** The existing literature on the limiting distributions of estimators for high-dimensional factor models focuses on covariance-based methods, leaving a gap for autocovariance-based approaches. Future research is needed to derive the limiting distributions for weight-calibrated autocovariance-based estimators, potentially leveraging results from reduced-rank regression.

**Why It Matters:** It addresses the fundamental need for statistical inference (confidence intervals, hypothesis testing) for the proposed estimators, which is critical for making them useful in rigorous empirical analysis.
