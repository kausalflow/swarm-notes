title: Theoretical Reachability of Flows
---

**Background:** While many generative models are empirically grounded in ODEs or SDEs, the exact class of target probability measures that a specific neural architecture can represent through flow matching is often not fully understood.

**Question / Future Work:** Bridging the gap between the empirical performance of practical training objectives in path-space flow matching and the formal theoretical capacity to represent arbitrary target path distributions remains a critical open problem.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing theoretical reachability is crucial for understanding the limitations of continuous-time generative frameworks and providing formal guarantees.

**Evidence:** a sharper theory of which path-space distributional maps are reachable by the practical training is desirable.
