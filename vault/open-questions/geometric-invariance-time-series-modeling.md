title: Geometric Invariance Modeling
---

**Background:** Time series classification models often struggle with tasks where input sequences lack the strict causal-temporal structure assumed by standard architectures.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop specialized feature extraction and representation learning modules that can effectively process non-causal or geometric temporal data without relying on temporal causal priors, ensuring foundation models can generalize beyond strictly causal domains.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a performance bottleneck where foundation models optimized for causal time series fail when applied to non-causal geometric data, limiting their general applicability.
