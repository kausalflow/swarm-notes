title: Optimal System of Generators Selection
---

**Background:** The performance of Generalized Covariance (GCov) specification tests for non-Gaussian time series depends on the selection and orthonormalization of a system of generators for nonlinear transformations.

**Question / Future Work:** Identifying tractable, efficient, and well-regularized methods for selecting optimal systems of generators for nonlinear transformations remains an ill-posed computational challenge in high-dimensional settings.

**Why It Matters:** This bottleneck directly limits the diagnostic power and efficiency of GCov-based tests in broader applications.

**Evidence:** In practice, the Post’s inversion formula does not provide a tractable means of inverting the Laplace transform, and it is an ill-posed problem.
