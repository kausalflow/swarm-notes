title: Quantum Attention Regime Robustness
---

**Background:** Financial markets are inherently non-stationary, and machine learning models often struggle with generalization when faced with shifting regimes in DeFi environments.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to understand how quantum-enhanced attention modules maintain robustness across diverse and changing market conditions, particularly concerning the stability of quantum feature encodings and variational circuit parameters under long-term regime shifts.

**Why It Matters:** Assessing long-term stability is critical for deploying automated rebalancing agents in real-world DeFi liquidity provision, where failure to adapt to new regimes can lead to significant financial loss.
