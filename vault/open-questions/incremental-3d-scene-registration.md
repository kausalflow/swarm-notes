title: Incremental 3D Scene Registration
---

**Background:** Human activities frequently occur in dynamic environments where users accumulate persistent 3D spatial knowledge, yet most motion prediction models rely on precomputed or static 3D scene inputs.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing a model capable of incrementally registering and updating 3D environmental knowledge during egocentric observation remains a significant challenge for proactive assistance systems. Integrating such dynamic 3D world-modeling is essential for robust motion forecasting that adapts to changing scenes.

**Why It Matters:** Current reliance on fixed 3D scene data is a fundamental bottleneck for deploying motion forecasting in open-world, long-term embodied AI applications.
