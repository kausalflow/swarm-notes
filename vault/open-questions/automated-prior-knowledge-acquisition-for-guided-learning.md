title: Automated prior knowledge acquisition
---

**Background:** Explanation-guided learning relies on expert-provided knowledge or approximations to constrain model behavior. Improving methods to learn these priors without manual input is a key challenge for widespread deployment.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed into methods for automatically learning or approximating prior functional knowledge, particularly in settings where explicit expert knowledge is unavailable, to ensure that constraints do not degrade predictive performance due to poor initial fits.

**Why It Matters:** Developing automated ways to establish priors is essential for the scalability and practical utility of explanation-guided training methods.
