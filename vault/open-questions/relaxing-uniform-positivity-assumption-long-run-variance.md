title: Relaxing long-run variance positivity
---

**Background:** High-dimensional Gaussian approximations for functional data typically require the assumption of uniform positivity of the long-run variance across the functional domain.

**Question / Future Work:** Formally relaxing the requirement of uniform positivity for the long-run variance in high-dimensional Gaussian approximations for functional time series.

**Why It Matters:** Relaxing this assumption would generalize the inference framework to regimes where the long-run variance might vanish at some points, increasing the practical robustness of the method.
