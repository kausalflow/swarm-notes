title: Optimal input sequence length
---

**Background:** Battery degradation trajectory forecasting (BDTF) models often process cumulative sequences of historical data as input, where the optimal duration of the observation window is not well-defined.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a fundamental challenge in identifying the optimal observation window length for operational time-series inputs, as longer history does not always improve prediction and can introduce redundancy, noise, or optimization instability.

**Why It Matters:** Optimizing input sequence lengths is crucial for balancing model accuracy with computational constraints and data availability in real-time management systems.
