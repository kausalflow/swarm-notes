title: Extending KARC to stochastic systems
---

**Background:** Forecasting dynamical systems often involves handling noise and stochastic perturbations that frequently arise in real-world physical processes. Deterministic reservoir computing models may face limitations when applied to such noisy environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending the KARC methodology to incorporate stochastic systems driven by noise is necessary for applicability in real-world scenarios where stochastic dynamics are central.

**Why It Matters:** Expanding the scope to stochastic dynamics is essential for its applicability in non-idealized environments, which is a recurring bottleneck for dynamical system modeling.

**Evidence:** Future works include to extend KARC to stochastic systems driven by noise.
