**Background:** Effective integration of machine learning forecasts with bike-sharing system simulations is crucial for deriving actionable strategic and tactical insights.

**Question / Future Work:** Further investigating the relationship between forecasting methodologies (like net demand prediction) and the downstream simulation of bike-sharing system dynamics, including relocation logistics, remains an area for future research.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding how forecast errors propagate through complex simulation models that drive operational planning is key to justifying the adoption of advanced ML techniques in real-world BSS management.

**Evidence:** Further investigating the relationship between forecasting and BSS simulation is an area of future research.
