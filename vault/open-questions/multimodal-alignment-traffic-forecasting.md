title: Improving Multimodal Data Alignment
---

**Background:** Cellular traffic is influenced by both intrinsic spatiotemporal dynamics and exogenous event-driven signals, requiring robust alignment between traffic observations and external data.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing sophisticated cross-modal alignment methods is crucial for enhancing the robustness of traffic forecasting under external disturbances when synchronizing cellular traffic signals with heterogeneous event streams.

**Why It Matters:** Effective multimodal fusion relies on accurate data alignment; without it, models cannot fully exploit the contextual information necessary for predicting event-driven traffic anomalies.
