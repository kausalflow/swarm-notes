title: Long-term Semantic Validation Updates
---

**Background:** Industrial processes often feature evolving instrumentation and measurement chains that can render static process documentation and pre-extracted semantic priors obsolete over time.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed into methods for long-term semantic validation and dynamic updates to credibility-correction mechanisms, ensuring that semantic priors remain synchronized with physical reality as sensors drift or process infrastructure is modified.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the bottleneck of maintaining trust in AI systems that rely on potentially static knowledge bases in highly dynamic and aging industrial environments.

**Evidence:** Future work will study long-term semantic validation and credibility-correction updates when measurement chains change.
