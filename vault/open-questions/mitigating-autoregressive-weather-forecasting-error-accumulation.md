title: Mitigating Autoregressive Forecasting Error Accumulation
---

**Background:** Autoregressive AI-based weather forecasting models frequently suffer from the progressive accumulation of prediction errors over extended temporal horizons.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing effective temporal consistency constraints or incorporating diffusion-based generative processes is required to mitigate error propagation in long-term atmospheric forecasting. Investigating these methods is essential to improve the stability and reliability of autoregressive models over multiple lead times.

**Why It Matters:** Error accumulation is a fundamental bottleneck for autoregressive AI weather models; solving it is critical for extending the reliable lead time of data-driven forecasting systems.
