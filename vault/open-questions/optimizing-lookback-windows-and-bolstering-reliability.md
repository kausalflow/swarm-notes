**Background:** Developing effective models for long-term time series forecasting requires optimizing the utilization of historical data, often necessitating a trade-off between lookback window size and computational cost.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work includes optimizing the lookback windows used by the model to further improve forecast accuracy and exploring methods to bolster forecast reliability specifically for deployment in operational settings.
