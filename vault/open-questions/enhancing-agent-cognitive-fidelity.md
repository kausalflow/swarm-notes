title: Enhancing Agent Cognitive Fidelity
---

**Background:** Multi-agent simulations often employ simplified cognitive models that fail to capture the full complexity of human decision-making in high-stakes environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing more sophisticated, psychologically-grounded reasoning architectures for agents is necessary to improve the predictive fidelity of socio-technical simulations beyond simple belief updates.

**Why It Matters:** Over-simplified cognitive models limit the ability of simulations to capture emergent social phenomena and real-world behavioral nuances.

**Evidence:** Limitations arise from simplified cognition, abstracted platform algorithms and network scales.
