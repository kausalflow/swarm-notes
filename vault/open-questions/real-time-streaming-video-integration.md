title: Integrate into Real-Time Video System
---

**Background:** The Foveated Diffusion framework lays the groundwork for efficient, gaze-contingent visual generation, particularly for interactive or streaming applications.

**Question / Future Work:** Integrating the developed Foveated Diffusion model into a real-time, streaming autoregressive video generation system, likely one incorporating an eye tracker, is identified as a compelling and impactful direction for future work.
