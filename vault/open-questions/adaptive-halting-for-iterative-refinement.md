title: Adaptive halting for refinement
---

**Background:** Adaptive computation time mechanisms allow neural networks to dynamically adjust their computational depth during inference based on the input.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing adaptive halting criteria for iterative refinement mechanisms in time series models to dynamically determine the optimal depth per sample, optimizing the trade-off between inference accuracy and computational cost.

**Why It Matters:** Static computational depth in iterative refinement models often leads to inefficiency; dynamic halting is a critical research direction for balancing accuracy with real-time requirements.
