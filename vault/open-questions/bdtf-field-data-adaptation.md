title: Adaptation to noisy field data
---

**Background:** Machine learning models for battery degradation are typically validated on controlled laboratory datasets, yet real-world field data—such as electric vehicle battery logs—are inherently noisy, irregular, and sparse.

**Question / Future Work:** The adaptation of advanced life-cycle forecasting frameworks to robustly process noisy, irregular, and sparse field-collected operational data remains a significant unresolved research problem for deployment.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between laboratory-validated prognostic models and in-the-wild deployment is essential for practical safety and diagnostics.
