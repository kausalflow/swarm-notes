title: Adaptive Spatial Structure Modeling
---

**Background:** Spatiotemporal traffic forecasting models are typically evaluated on fixed, static graph structures representing road networks, which may not capture the dynamic nature of changing traffic conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing spatial modeling mechanisms where the graph topology or the spatial propagation structure dynamically evolves in response to observed traffic patterns, rather than relying on pre-defined static graphs.

**Why It Matters:** Real-world traffic flow is inherently dynamic; a model capable of adapting its spatial dependency structure to changing conditions offers potential for improved robustness and generalization across traffic regimes.
