title: Scaling Recursive Emulation to Video
---

**Background:** Generative models for dynamical systems often face a trade-off between computational efficiency and physical accuracy. While recursive techniques can bridge this gap by enforcing consistency across discretization scales, scaling these approaches to high-complexity natural video remains an unsolved challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the scalability of recursive flow matching frameworks beyond well-defined physical systems to complex, high-dimensional natural video domains, where rich semantic and temporal variations introduce additional modeling complexity.

**Why It Matters:** Extending high-efficiency generative emulation from controlled scientific domains to unconstrained natural video is a critical step for developing foundation models for real-world dynamics.
