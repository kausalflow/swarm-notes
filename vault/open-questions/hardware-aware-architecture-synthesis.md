title: Hardware-Aware Architecture Synthesis
---

**Background:** Neural architecture search often optimizes for predictive metrics, failing to account for the physical constraints of deployment hardware.

**Question / Future Work:** Research into incorporating specific latency, energy consumption, and memory footprint constraints directly into architecture generation prompts is needed to foster the development of 'Green AI' models for edge-computing IoT devices.

**Why It Matters:** Enforcing hardware awareness is a prerequisite for moving beyond general-purpose models to deployment-specific, resource-efficient architectures.

**Evidence:** Future research will explore Hardware-Aware Synthesis, to enable the prompt to include constraints of performance in regard to latency and memory, making available "Green AI" designs optimized for edge-computing IoT devices.
