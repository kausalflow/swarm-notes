title: Path Invariance Under Capacity Constraints
---

**Background:** Resource-constrained project management models, such as the capacity-aware Parr formulation, rely on the assumption that total effort and progress trajectories remain stable under varying capacity levels.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating whether capacity constraints preserve path invariance or introduce distinct organizational inefficiencies is essential for validating the use of latent effort curves in volatile resourcing scenarios.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the theoretical limitations of decoupling latent effort from operational execution under resource scarcity.

**Evidence:** it’s hard to forecast if that increase will preserve some sort of path invariance or will introduce inefficiencies
