title: Robustness in Industrial RUL Estimation
---

**Background:** Industrial sensor streams are inherently heterogeneous and prone to missing data or varying operational regimes.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop robust techniques for RUL estimation that can handle missing sensor data, significant operating-regime shifts, and scenarios with limited labeled data, while improving the tokenization and channel handling for heterogeneous sensor inputs.

**Why It Matters:** Real-world industrial environments are complex and dynamic; models that fail under these conditions lack the necessary generalizability for practical deployment.

**Evidence:** (ii) improve multivariate tokenization and channel handling for heterogeneous sensors, and (iii) evaluate robustness under missing sensors, operating-regime shifts, and low-label settings.
