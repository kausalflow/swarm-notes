title: Latent Dynamics for Safe Planning
---

**Background:** Learned latent dynamics models for aerial robotics prioritize task performance but often lack native mechanisms to incorporate safety constraints directly into the latent space or the optimization loop.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating methods to encode safety-relevant structures (e.g., obstacle proximity, visibility, state recoverability) directly within the latent representation or predictive planning objective.

**Why It Matters:** Explicit safety integration is critical for transitioning from laboratory control tasks to robust deployment in dynamic, hazardous environments.

**Evidence:** A second direction is to incorporate safety more explicitly into the learned representation and planning objective.
