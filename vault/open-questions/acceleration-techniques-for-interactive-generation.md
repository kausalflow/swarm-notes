title: Future acceleration techniques

**Background:** Autoregressive video generation models require efficient mechanisms to reduce computational overhead during long sequence synthesis to enable faster generation and improved interactivity.

**Question / Future Work:** Although the proposed model achieves 16 FPS, further acceleration is desired for even better interactive experiences. Future research should investigate integrating techniques like sparse attention and attention sink into the current architecture to achieve faster generation speeds.
