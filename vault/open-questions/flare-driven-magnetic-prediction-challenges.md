title: Flare-Driven Magnetic Field Prediction
---

**Background:** Solar flare events induce rapid, small-scale magnetic restructuring that current data-driven forecasting models often struggle to capture. MSE-based optimization, commonly used in these models, frequently results in over-smoothed predictions that miss impulsive dynamics critical for space weather.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop flare-aware loss functions, integrate auxiliary multi-modal inputs such as X-ray and EUV data, and utilize adversarial training objectives to better preserve sharp structural features during impulsive solar events. Expanding the benchmark sets to include high-flare-activity samples is necessary for operational validation.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential because the most severe space weather impacts are linked to rapid eruptive events that current models currently underpredict.
