title: Intervention-aware Koopman Modeling
---

**Background:** Nonstationary data streams often contain abrupt pattern shifts, making it difficult for models to maintain accurate forecasts without re-learning from scratch.

**Question / Future Work:** Incorporating exogenous inputs or interventions into Koopman operator regression frameworks remains an unresolved challenge, limiting their application in systems driven by control or causality.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for extending dynamical system modeling to real-world applications involving active interventions or external control.
