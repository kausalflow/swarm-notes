title: Robustness to Non-Stationarity
---

**Background:** The established test relies on the strong consistency of the estimator under the assumption of a stationary and square-integrable Markov process. The empirical work uses log-returns, which are often modeled as stationary, but real-world financial data may exhibit non-stationarity or structural breaks.

**Question / Future Work:** Extend the consistency and size properties of the M-vine Granger causality in expectiles test to include processes that may be conditionally heteroskedastic, non-stationary, or subject to occasional structural breaks, which are common in financial and macroeconomic time series.
