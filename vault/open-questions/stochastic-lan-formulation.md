title: Stochastic LAN formulation
---

**Background:** Stochastic differential equations (SDEs) allow for the modeling of systems with intrinsic random noise, providing a framework for uncertainty quantification in dynamical processes.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research could explore replacing the deterministic LAN-ODE formulation with a stochastic differential equation (SDE) approach to inherently incorporate uncertainty quantification into the attention mechanism.

**Why It Matters:** Uncertainty quantification is a critical requirement for continuous-time models in safety-critical applications like autonomous control.

**Evidence:** Replacing the LAN-ODE with stochastic differential equation (SDE) is a natural direction for future work, as it will introduce intrinsic uncertainty quantification into the attention mechanism.
