title: Stellar-BH Cluster Interaction
---

**Background:** The evolution of star clusters involves processes like binary hardening, which converts binding energy into heat that can drive core collapse and runaway collisions.

**Question / Future Work:** The interaction of stellar mass black holes (formed from massive stars) with the rest of the cluster, specifically how this affects subsequent evolution after black hole seed formation, needs further detailed exploration.
