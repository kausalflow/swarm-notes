title: Extreme precipitation underestimation
---

**Background:** Data-driven weather forecasting models often systematically underestimate high-intensity precipitation extremes.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing techniques to improve the capture of extreme precipitation peaks without sacrificing the spatial or temporal coherence gained by downscaling models remains a significant challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Extreme precipitation events are high-impact phenomena; addressing the systematic underestimation bias is essential for the reliability of operational forecasts.
