title: Comparative Power in Tails
---

**Background:** Expectile-based Granger causality measures are designed to capture dependence in various parts of the distribution, including extreme risk regions (tails). The M-vine test is applied to financial return data, which are known for tail dependence.

**Question / Future Work:** Examine the performance and comparative power of the M-vine Granger causality in expectiles test against alternative tail-dependence measures or tests designed specifically for extreme quantiles or expectiles, especially in high-dimensional or non-stationary financial time series contexts.
