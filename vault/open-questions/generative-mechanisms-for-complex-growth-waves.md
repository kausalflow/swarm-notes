title: Generative mechanisms for growth waves
---

**Background:** The generative mechanisms underlying complex growth structures in financial time series, such as multi-component sigmoid stacks, remain phenomenologically observed rather than theoretically explained.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to derive a generative model that explains the specific multi-component wave structure observed in assets like Bitcoin, distinguishing it from spurious noise and clarifying if the structure arises from adoption dynamics, protocol events, or macroeconomic feedback.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding these mechanisms is necessary to distinguish between structural properties of asset adoption and transient historical artifacts.

**Evidence:** The wave structure is a phenomenological observation that constrains what any future explanation must accommodate.
