title: Finite-sample Conformal Validity
---

**Background:** Conformal prediction methods for time series generally lack unconditional finite-sample distribution-free coverage guarantees due to violations of exchangeability caused by serial dependence.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open challenge to develop a formal, unconditional finite-sample coverage guarantee for conformal time-series predictors that maintain efficiency. The field currently struggles to bridge the gap between empirical coverage in non-exchangeable, non-stationary settings and rigorous theoretical guarantees.

**Why It Matters:** The lack of formal coverage guarantees is a primary bottleneck for deploying probabilistic time-series forecasting in high-stakes environments where calibration failure can have severe safety consequences.
