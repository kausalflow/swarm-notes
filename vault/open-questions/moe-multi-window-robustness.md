title: MoE multi-window robustness
---

**Background:** Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures in reinforcement learning facilitate multi-objective task handling but often exhibit performance degradation when specialized experts are forced to operate outside their primary time horizon.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is needed to determine how expert specialization interacts with temporal scales and whether specific multi-horizon training objectives can ensure robust performance for both short-term momentum and long-term investment goals within a single model.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing the degradation of expert-based policies over varying investment horizons is essential for creating reliable, retail-scale portfolio managers.
