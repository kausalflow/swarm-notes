title: Adaptive Segmentation for Streaming Transformers
---

**Background:** Real-time classification of physiological time-series data requires balancing local temporal modeling with long-term context integration on constrained hardware.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop adaptive segmentation strategies that transcend fixed-length windows, enabling models to dynamically adjust their temporal resolution based on signal dynamics or physiological events while maintaining low-latency inference.

**Why It Matters:** Fixed-window approaches may miss transient physiological events, creating a bottleneck for diagnostic accuracy in real-time edge monitoring systems.
