title: Robustness of Agentic Climate Pipelines
---

**Background:** Agentic pipelines rely on upstream data and analytical tools whose inherent biases, spatiotemporal gaps, and missing data patterns can propagate errors throughout a multi-step reasoning process.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should investigate methods for enhancing the robustness of agentic climate pipelines to handle data inconsistencies and tool failures. This includes developing uncertainty-aware and scenario-driven analytical tools that can communicate data limitations explicitly to stakeholders during high-stakes climate policy discussions.

**Why It Matters:** Agentic tool reliability is a significant bottleneck for deploying automated assistants in real-world, high-stakes decision support environments.
