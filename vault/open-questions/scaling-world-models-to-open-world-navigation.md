title: Scaling World Models to Open-World
---

**Background:** Navigation world models depend on training on limited robotics datasets, often failing when deployed in unseen environments.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved challenge in scaling navigation world models to arbitrary, large-scale, and highly dynamic environments that fall outside the distribution of current training datasets. Future research needs to explore better foundation-model initialization and continuous adaptation methods to bridge this generalization gap.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization is a fundamental bottleneck for embodied agents, limiting their deployment in non-controlled, real-world settings.

**Evidence:** it remains unclear how to scale these models to arbitrary, large-scale, and highly dynamic open-world environments that fall outside the distribution of current robotics training datasets.
