title: Cross-survey representation generalization
---

**Background:** Self-supervised representation learning models trained on one astronomical catalog often demonstrate performance degradation when applied to different observational domains or telescope surveys due to domain shift and differing sampling characteristics.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing methods for the effective cross-survey transfer learning of representations pre-trained on large-scale catalogs is critical for scaling across next-generation observatories without requiring extensive retraining.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization across surveys is vital for creating universal foundational models in astronomy, reducing the overhead of bespoke model development.
