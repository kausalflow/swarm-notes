title: Probabilistic Ensemble Forecasting Extension
---

**Background:** Standard meta-learning approaches in time series forecasting primarily focus on producing point forecasts rather than capturing the full probability distribution of future outcomes.

**Question / Future Work:** Adapting multi-task meta-learning frameworks designed for point-forecasting to output interval forecasts or full probability density combinations remains a substantial unresolved bottleneck for robust decision-making.

**Why It Matters:** Transitioning from point forecasts to probabilistic or density forecasting is critical for better risk assessment in real-world applications.

**Evidence:** As a drawback, the proposed method is limited to producing only point forecasts; this motivates future research in the direction of adapting it to output interval forecasts.
