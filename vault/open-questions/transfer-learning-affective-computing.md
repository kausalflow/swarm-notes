title: Optimizing foundation model transfer
---

**Background:** Foundation models for video understanding demonstrate potential for cross-domain and cross-taxonomy transfer in affective computing, yet systematic strategies for leveraging these capabilities are under-researched.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematic evaluation of fine-tuning objectives, curriculum learning, and multi-stage fine-tuning is required to optimize foundation model transfer across diverse emotional taxonomies and domains.

**Why It Matters:** Optimizing transfer learning reduces reliance on large-scale, human-annotated datasets for novel affective computing tasks.

**Evidence:** While preliminary results suggest cross-dataset and cross-taxonomy transfer... a more systematic study is needed to understand how Video2Reaction can support foundation models that generalize across domains and taxonomies.
