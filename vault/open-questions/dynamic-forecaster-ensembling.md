title: Dynamic ensemble strategies for EPF
---

**Background:** Electricity price forecasting models are increasingly blending statistical methods with modern machine learning, such as time series foundation models. There is a need to understand how to optimally combine these fundamentally different approaches to leverage their complementary strengths.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing advanced ensemble strategies that dynamically determine when and under which conditions specific models (e.g., based on performance, volatility, or regime indicators) should be prioritized in electricity price forecasting is a critical research gap.

**Why It Matters:** Developing robust ensemble strategies is critical for improving the reliability and accuracy of electricity price forecasts in complex market conditions, moving beyond simple averaging.
