title: Extending Regex Time Series Search
---

**Background:** Regular-expression-inspired query languages for time series provide a flexible alternative to traditional sequence-based similarity matching.

**Question / Future Work:** Generalizing regex-based query frameworks to handle variable-rate sampling, streaming data streams, and approximate similarity search (k-NN) remains an open challenge for distributed time-series analysis.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the limitation of current exact, static-focused approaches, enabling broader utility in real-time and heterogeneous streaming environments.

**Evidence:** Future work includes variable-rate sampling and combining exact pattern retrieval with approximate k-NN search.
