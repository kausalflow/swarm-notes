title: Full-lifecycle Gaussian pre-training
---

**Background:** 3D Gaussian-centric representations have emerged as an efficient and expressive approach for scene modeling in autonomous driving, but self-supervised pre-training strategies covering the full lifecycle—from perception to temporal forecasting and motion planning—remain largely unexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing comprehensive self-supervised pre-training frameworks tailored for the full end-to-end lifecycle of Gaussian-centric autonomous driving models, particularly those integrating perception, 4D forecasting, and motion planning, remains a significant open challenge.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for reducing reliance on manual annotations and establishing foundational models that generalize across perception, temporal forecasting, and planning in autonomous systems.

**Evidence:** Still, a comprehensive self-supervised pre-training strategy tailored for the full lifecycle of Gaussian-centric models remains largely unexplored.
