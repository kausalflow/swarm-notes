title: Multivariate VLM-as-a-Judge Evaluation
---

**Background:** Multivariate time series forecasting involves complex cross-variable interactions that currently lack a standardized VLM-based evaluation approach. Existing frameworks focus primarily on univariate series, where mapping to 2D plots is straightforward.

**Question / Future Work:** Researching effective visualization designs to represent inter-variable dependencies and developing robust rubrics that allow VLMs to perform multi-output, cross-variable reasoning for joint forecasting evaluation.

**Why It Matters:** Multivariate forecasting is standard in industry and climate applications; limiting VLM evaluation to univariate plots restricts its practical utility.
