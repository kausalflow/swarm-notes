title: Adaptive Fusion Non-Linearity Potential
---

**Background:** Adaptive Frequency Fusion (AFF) dynamically weights contributions from event-driven (textual) and numerical prediction branches across frequency bands to leverage their complementary strengths.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining whether alternative, more complex non-linear fusion strategies (like MLP or Cross-Attention fusion) might eventually surpass the parsimonious 6-parameter learnable weights used in Adaptive Frequency Fusion (AFF) on datasets exhibiting extreme or unusual frequency component distributions requires further investigation, especially as the model generalizes to unseen distribution shifts.
