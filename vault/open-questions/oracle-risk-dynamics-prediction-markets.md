title: Oracle risk dynamics and behavior
---

**Background:** Decentralized prediction markets rely on oracles to resolve the outcomes of traded claims.

**Question / Future Work:** Understanding the mechanisms and behavioral dynamics surrounding oracle resolution events, such as disputes and potential manipulation, is essential for ensuring market resilience and credibility. Future research should characterize the economic and behavioral drivers of these risk events and develop robust frameworks to mitigate the impact of oracle failures.

**Why It Matters:** Oracle resolution is a critical point of failure in decentralized prediction markets, making understanding the dynamics around it essential for market design.
