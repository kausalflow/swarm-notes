title: Causal Inference in V-NSDEs
---

**Background:** Policy-counterfactual analysis and causal inference are essential for evaluating the impact of interventions on dynamical systems, yet traditional V-NSDE models lack explicit mechanisms to handle these tasks.

**Question / Future Work:** Exploring the integration of causal constraints and the development of policy-counterfactual simulation methods within the V-NSDE framework is necessary to move beyond descriptive modeling toward actionable decision-support tools.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between descriptive stochastic modeling and causal counterfactual analysis is vital for moving from mere trend prediction to prescriptive evidence-based planning.
