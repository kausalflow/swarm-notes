title: Robustness of M-vine Causality Test
---

**Background:** Developing robust statistical tests for causal inference in time series is an ongoing area of research, especially when dealing with complex non-linear and non-Gaussian dependencies.

**Question / Future Work:** Further investigation is required to assess the performance and applicability of the M-vine test for Granger causality in expectiles when the conditioning variables $\mathbf{Z}_t$ are themselves subject to misspecification in the M-vine modeling step, or when the time series exhibits long-range dependence not fully captured by the assumed Markov structure.
