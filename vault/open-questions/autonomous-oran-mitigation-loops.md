title: Closing the autonomous loop
---

**Background:** Anomaly detection frameworks in O-RAN generate diagnostic alerts that currently require manual intervention for root-cause analysis and mitigation.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to bridge diagnostic anomaly detection systems into autonomous, closed-loop frameworks that automatically leverage identified root causes to generate and apply corrective network policies.

**Why It Matters:** Autonomous closed-loop operation is a fundamental requirement for self-healing networks.

**Evidence:** A compelling extension is to close the loop by using this information to identify the root cause and generate policies to address the identified anomaly.
