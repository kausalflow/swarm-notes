title: Topology-Aware Forecasting and Evaluation
---

**Background:** Time series forecasting evaluation traditionally relies on pointwise error metrics like Mean Squared Error, which fail to capture structural properties such as recurrent dynamics, oscillatory behavior, and phase alignment.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is needed to extend topological evaluation to higher-dimensional homology, integrate uncertainty quantification into structural assessment, and transition from evaluative metrics to topology-aware training objectives.

**Why It Matters:** These directions are critical for moving beyond post-hoc diagnostic tools toward models explicitly trained to preserve structural signal integrity.

**Evidence:** Future work includes higher homology dimensions, probabilistic forecasts, and topology-aware training objectives.
