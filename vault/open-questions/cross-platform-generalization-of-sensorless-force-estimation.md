title: Sensorless Estimation Generalization Limits
---

**Background:** Sensorless wrench estimation maps proprioceptive state histories to interaction forces, yet these models are often tuned to specific mechanical platforms and operational conditions.

**Question / Future Work:** Establish comprehensive benchmarking protocols to assess the cross-platform and cross-task transferability of sensorless force estimation models in robotic environments.

**Why It Matters:** Determining whether these models learn universal physical dynamics or platform-specific noise is critical for the scalability of robot-environment interaction systems.

**Evidence:** the evaluation is limited to a single hydraulic manipulator and excavation setting, and broader generalization across contact objects, robot platforms, and tasks remains to be validated.
