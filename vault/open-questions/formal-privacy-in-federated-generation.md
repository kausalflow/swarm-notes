title: Formal Privacy in Federated Generation
---

**Background:** Synthetic data generation in privacy-preserving environments often faces a trade-off between generative fidelity and formal privacy guarantees.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is required to integrate and evaluate formal privacy-preserving mechanisms, such as differential privacy, within federated generative workflows without excessively compromising data utility.

**Why It Matters:** Mathematical privacy guarantees are essential for the widespread, ethical adoption of synthetic data generation in clinical collaborations.
