title: Scaling Clinical Timeline Annotation
---

**Background:** Clinical timeline reconstruction methods often face a trade-off between the semantic richness of narrative clinical notes and the temporal precision of structured electronic health record data. Achieving high event recovery rates alongside precise temporal localization in large-scale clinical settings remains a persistent challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research needs to determine the generalizability of multistep graph-based reconstruction beyond specific conditions like sepsis and evaluate the framework's performance degradation when multimodal data is incomplete or unavailable.

**Why It Matters:** Exposes the bottleneck of multimodal dependency in automated clinical trajectory modeling.
