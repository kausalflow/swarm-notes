title: Lightweight edge multimodal perception
---

**Background:** Edge adaptation modules for VLA-based navigation must balance low computational overhead with high-level environmental reasoning to maintain safety.

**Question / Future Work:** While the existing system uses LiDAR-based proximity, there is a need to integrate lightweight, edge-based multimodal local perception models. This would enhance the system's safety redundancy and allow the robot to better understand and react to dynamic environmental features that are not captured by simple geometric constraints alone.

**Why It Matters:** As VLA models become more capable, the edge adapter must evolve to process richer local context to safely handle increasingly complex and crowded dynamic environments.

**Evidence:** investigate the introduction of lightweight multimodal local perception models at the edge to further enhance the system’s safety redundancy and robustness in dynamic scenarios.
