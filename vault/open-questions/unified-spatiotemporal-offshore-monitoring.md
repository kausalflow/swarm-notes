title: Unified Spatiotemporal Monitoring Framework
---

**Background:** Offshore wind infrastructure monitoring relies on spatial detection to identify turbine locations before temporal analysis of backscatter profiles can occur.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved need to integrate spatial localization and temporal analysis into a unified spatiotemporal framework to enable early-onset detection of construction phases without relying on prior location knowledge.

**Why It Matters:** Improving early-onset detection is critical for accurate monitoring of the entire construction lifecycle, which is currently hindered by the two-stage separation of spatial detection and temporal analysis.

**Evidence:** Early detection without prior location knowledge remains a conceptually unresolved problem.
