title: ReIMTS Compatibility with ODE Models
---

**Background:** The recursive multi-scale framework ReIMTS is designed to be compatible with most existing encoder-decoder models for Irregular Multivariate Time Series (IMTS) forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the theoretical explainability and practical compatibility of extending the ReIMTS recursive multi-scale framework to integrate with ODE-based IMTS forecasting models, which may require specific theoretical justifications for the integration of their continuous-time representations.
