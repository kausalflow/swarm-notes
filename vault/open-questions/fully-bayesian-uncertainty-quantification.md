title: Bayesian flow field treatment
---

**Background:** The current framework provides point forecasts and prediction intervals based on empirical cross-validation error distributions, but does not explicitly account for uncertainty in the estimated flow field parameters themselves.

**Question / Future Work:** A natural extension involves developing a fully Bayesian treatment for the flow-field model, which would allow for uncertainty quantification that explicitly accounts for parameter uncertainty in the speed function, trajectory functions, and relaxation rates.
