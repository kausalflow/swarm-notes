title: Robust fMRI Conditioning Strategies
---

**Background:** Rest-to-task fMRI synthesis models often rely on conditioning mechanisms to map resting-state brain dynamics to specific task-evoked activity.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is needed to develop more flexible conditioning strategies that robustly map resting-state dynamics to diverse, heterogeneous experimental task paradigms while ensuring physiological validity of the synthesized signals.

**Why It Matters:** Effective conditioning is the primary bottleneck for scaling fMRI synthesis across clinical and cognitive experiments with varying temporal event structures.
