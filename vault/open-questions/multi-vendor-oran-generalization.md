title: Multi-vendor O-RAN validation
---

**Background:** Multi-vendor O-RAN deployments are characterized by heterogeneous hardware and software configurations, complicating the creation of universally applicable anomaly detection baselines.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to validate if zero-shot multi-modal anomaly detection frameworks maintain performance across heterogeneous, multi-vendor O-RAN environments as opposed to single, controlled testbed implementations.

**Why It Matters:** Ensuring generalizability across the fragmented O-RAN ecosystem is critical for practical operational adoption.

**Evidence:** A natural next step is to validate DAST across heterogeneous, multi-vendor O-RAN deployments and expand the performance degradation patterns considered in our evaluation.
