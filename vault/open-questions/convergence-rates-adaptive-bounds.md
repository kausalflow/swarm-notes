title: Convergence Rates for Adaptive Bounds
---

**Background:** Recursive Bayesian procedures use non-negative decreasing sequences of bounds to detect stationarity and assess series convergence.

**Question / Future Work:** Establishing rigorous theoretical convergence rates for nonparametric adaptive bound construction is necessary for the reliability of these procedures in high-stakes online learning.

**Why It Matters:** Lack of convergence guarantees limits the trust and safety of recursive Bayesian methods in sensitive online learning applications.
