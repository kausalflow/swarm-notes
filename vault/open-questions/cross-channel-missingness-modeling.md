title: Cross-Channel Missingness Modeling
---

**Background:** Multivariate time series forecasting frequently encounters missing data patterns that are correlated across channels due to systemic events like sensor network failures or power outages.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should focus on explicit cross-channel dependency modeling for both observational existence and value evolution, moving beyond implicit capture through individual masks to leverage structural priors inherent in systemic failure events.

**Why It Matters:** Explicitly modeling joint missingness across channels can significantly improve forecast accuracy and uncertainty estimation in multivariate systems.
