title: RL-Based Roundabout Control Robustness
---

**Background:** Automated vehicle speed advisory systems for roundabout entry must operate under partial observability caused by the non-deterministic intentions of human drivers. RL performance and robustness relative to model-based baselines under significant prediction uncertainty remains an open issue.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the feasibility and scalability of RL-based speed advisory systems in high-dynamic environments where decision-making must bridge the gap between model-based control and uncertain multi-agent future states.

**Why It Matters:** This question is foundational for evaluating the viability of RL for real-world deployment in mixed-traffic urban environments.
