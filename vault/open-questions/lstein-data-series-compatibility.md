title: Alternative Data Series Compatibility
---

**Background:** Data visualization techniques often struggle to effectively represent sparse higher-dimensional (2.5D) datasets, such as multi-passband photometric lightcurves, without introducing significant distortion or loss of critical information.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear whether there are additional data series types, beyond simple scatter and line plots, where the proposed projection framework could serve as a useful or valid visualization alternative. Investigating these possibilities and their respective projection requirements is necessary to expand the applicability of the visualization approach.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the limitations and potential breadth of compatible data types is crucial for establishing the framework as a general-purpose scientific visualization tool across different research domains.

**Evidence:** It has yet to be explored, if there are other series types besides scatter- and line-plots where LStein could be a useful alternative for visualisation.
