title: Detectability Limits in MTSAD
---

**Background:** The presence of structurally distinct anomaly types in multivariate time series necessitates different detection strategies. Current unsupervised methods lack a unified way to differentiate between algorithmic limitations and fundamental dataset-level anomaly detectability.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the fundamental limits of anomaly detectability in multivariate time-series datasets, and distinguishing between failures due to model architecture versus failures due to lack of detectable signal, remains an open problem.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding detectability limits prevents the pursuit of infeasible performance gains on fundamentally ambiguous datasets.
