title: Scaling Behavioral Simulation Bottlenecks
---

**Background:** Certain behavioral simulation tasks, such as those involving human cognitive heuristics or reward-based learning, show stagnant performance despite increases in model scale or standard fine-tuning.

**Question / Future Work:** There is an unresolved bottleneck in enabling LLMs to accurately simulate specific human cognitive behaviors and heuristics that appear orthogonal to general reasoning benchmarks. Investigation is needed to determine if this requires novel architectural changes, fundamentally different training objectives, or new approaches to modeling individual human idiosyncrasies beyond next-token prediction scaling.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for determining the limits of LLMs as faithful simulators of complex human cognition and behavioral patterns.
