title: Heavy-tailed distributions in HMMs
---

**Background:** Operational risk loss distributions often exhibit heavy-tailed characteristics, and the standard Gaussian distribution used in current Hidden Markov Model (HMM) frameworks may insufficiently capture these extreme behaviors.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should explore extending the current multivariate HMM framework to utilize heavy-tailed distributions, such as Student-t or other elliptical family distributions, to better model tail risk in operational loss data.

**Why It Matters:** Improving the tail risk representation is critical for accurate capital requirement estimation in banking, where underestimating extreme losses can lead to severe financial instability.
