title: Explore Alternative Gravity Parameterizations
---

**Background:** The theoretical framework developed in this study relies on a parameterization where deviations from General Relativity (GR) are encoded as an effective rescaling of the gravitational constant.

**Question / Future Work:** The simple linear rescaling of the gravitational constant is not the most informative parameterization for modified gravity in this context, motivating the exploration of more complex modified gravity parameterizations to better utilize the constraining power of upcoming surveys.
