title: Transferring GP Building Blocks
---

**Background:** Genetic programming approaches for feature learning are typically trained independently for each dataset, which is computationally intensive. Knowledge transfer between datasets remains an under-explored mechanism.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating mechanisms for transferring or reusing effective subtrees or structural building blocks discovered during the evolutionary process across related datasets could improve search efficiency and performance in data-constrained scenarios.

**Why It Matters:** Improving search efficiency through knowledge reuse is essential for scaling GP-based feature learning to broader domains.
