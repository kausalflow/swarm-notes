title: Efficient Transformer Edge Deployment
---

**Background:** Transformer-based forecasting models often face computational and memory constraints that limit their deployment on resource-constrained edge devices.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to develop more efficient versions of transformer-based forecasting models suitable for deployment in embedded systems. This involves investigating techniques such as lightweight attention approximations, model pruning, and model quantization to maintain performance while reducing the computational footprint.

**Why It Matters:** Enabling complex temporal models to operate on edge hardware is essential for decentralized energy management and real-time inference in remote locations.
