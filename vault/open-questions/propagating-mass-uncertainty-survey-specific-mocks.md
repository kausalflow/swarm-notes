title: Propagate Mass Uncertainty & Survey Mocks
---

**Background:** The study established a resource-efficient configuration for future surveys based on current findings, but this was based on specific assumed parameters.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should focus on propagating halo-mass uncertainties derived from weak-lensing calibration into the predicted gravitational redshift signal, and on configuring mock catalogues to match the specific selection functions and systematic effect budgets of individual planned surveys.
