title: Optimal Heterogeneous Lidar Fusion
---

**Background:** 4D FMCW Lidar provides instantaneous radial velocity information for each point, which is theoretically useful for dynamic scene understanding but introduces tradeoffs in spatial resolution and sensing range.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains an open problem to determine the optimal sensor fusion architecture that balances the dense geometric precision of conventional scanning Lidar with the motion-aware, instantaneous radial velocity measurements of 4D FMCW Lidar for various autonomous driving tasks. Establishing benchmarks to optimize this heterogeneous sensor synergy across different environmental conditions and dynamic scenarios is essential for future development.
