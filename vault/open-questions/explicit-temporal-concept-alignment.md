title: Explicit Temporal Concept Alignment
---

**Background:** Large language models often struggle to ground temporal numerical data into semantic linguistic concepts.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should move beyond task-specific auxiliary losses and explore comprehensive training curricula that allow models to learn structural temporal concepts like trend, periodicity, and level shifts directly from time-series-text pairs.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental limitation in grounding time-series data in language models; without explicit concept alignment, models lack robust reasoning capabilities for time-series analysis.
