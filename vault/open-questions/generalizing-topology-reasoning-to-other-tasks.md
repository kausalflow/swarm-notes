title: Generalizing Topology-based Reasoning Tasks
---

**Background:** Building knowledge graphs can be utilized to route exogenous variable selection for time-series forecasting, but their utility remains focused on forecasting rather than broader analytical tasks.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate whether the agentic topology-routed selection mechanism can be generalized beyond forecasting to other building management tasks such as virtual sensing, sensor outage recovery, anomaly detection, or fault localization.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing a unified approach to building intelligence reduces the need for task-specific models and pipelines.

**Evidence:** Extending topology-routed exogenous variable selection beyond forecasting is therefore an important direction for future work.
