title: Multivariate Cross-Transcript Analysis
---

**Background:** Transcripts describe local evolution within a univariate series, but can theoretically be extended to cross-series interactions.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending the current univariate transcript framework to cross-transcripts between multiple coupled time series is necessary to characterize complex synchronization regimes in multivariate data.

**Why It Matters:** This extension is critical for applying algebraic dependence testing to multivariate and networked systems.

**Evidence:** Future work along lines similar to the present work will explore two extensions of the above framework: transcripts between two or more time series (i. e., cross-transcripts) and transcripts between spatial ordinal patterns.
