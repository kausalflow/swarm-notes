title: Long-Time Limit of Driven Orders
---

**Background:** The theoretical analysis of periodically driven interacting many-body systems, performed in the large-N limit and coupled to a Markovian bath, neglects fluctuations that lead to long-time thermalization.

**Question / Future Work:** The true long-time limit for the single order parameter case, which has been discussed in related work, needs to be extended to the intertwined order parameter models analyzed here to fully characterize the eventual fate of the driven phases.
