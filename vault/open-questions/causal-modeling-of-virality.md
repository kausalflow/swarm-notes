title: Causal Modeling of Virality
---

**Background:** Current predictive models for meme virality are correlational in nature, quantifying the relationship between content or network features and engagement without uncovering the causal mechanisms.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to transition from purely predictive, associative models to causal or intervention-based frameworks that explicitly account for how platform exposure mechanisms, algorithmic ranking, and human behavior interact to drive viral cascades.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the causal drivers of virality is critical for designing platforms that can better manage information flow, moderate harmful content, and influence the spread of information.
