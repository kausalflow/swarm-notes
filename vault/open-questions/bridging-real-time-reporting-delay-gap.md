title: Addressing Real-Time Reporting Delays
---

**Background:** Epidemiological and genetic data used for forecasting are subject to real-time reporting delays that can impact the applicability of retrospective studies to operational forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** More work is needed to bridge the gap between retrospective study results (which ignore reporting delays) and the reality of real-time operational forecasting, especially for models that rely on genetic variant data which is known to have meaningful reporting lags.
