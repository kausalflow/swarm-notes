title: Adaptive Graph Filtering

**Background:** A plugin-based framework for multivariate time series forecasting utilizes graph spectral decomposition and Mixture-of-Experts routing to adaptively model dependencies across different frequency bands.

**Question / Future Work:** Further development could focus on creating more adaptive and interpretable graph filtering strategies, such as utilizing continuous expert blending or attention-based routing mechanisms, to enhance robustness and transparency.
