title: Online Adaptive Generative Paradigms
---

**Background:** Current generative time series models, particularly diffusion-based approaches, are often computationally intensive and designed for static datasets, creating a gap for deployment in dynamic environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing online adaptive generative paradigms that handle temporal distribution shifts with low latency is necessary to transition these models into real-time applications.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging this gap is essential for deploying advanced generative models in latency-sensitive, real-world production environments.

**Evidence:** Current generative time series models are mostly operated under the static environments and could have large computation burdens... How to develop online adaptive paradigms with tolerable latency could be another practical research direction.
