title: Multimodal Fusion Architecture Bottleneck
---

**Background:** Multimodal Earth observation datasets often combine heterogeneous data sources, yet designing architectures that fully exploit the complementary strengths of these modalities remains an open research challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** The integration of diverse sensors (e.g., optical vs. SAR, VHR vs. low-resolution time series) into a single deep learning pipeline presents significant difficulties in determining optimal fusion strategies, such as whether to apply early, mid-level, or late fusion. Existing results suggest that simply adding more modalities does not guarantee improved performance, pointing to the need for better architectural designs to handle multimodal input.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental bottleneck in remote sensing machine learning, as effective fusion is necessary to leverage the increasingly diverse EO data landscape.
