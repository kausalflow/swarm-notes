title: Biomedical Multi-label Threshold Robustness
---

**Background:** Deep learning models for multi-label classification of high-dimensional biomedical signals rely on decision thresholds for binary prediction that are typically optimized on training data.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating whether hybrid architectures—specifically those blending structural priors like U-Nets with sequence models like Mamba—can improve the robustness of diagnostic thresholds against distributional shifts across clinical sites and cohorts.

**Why It Matters:** Current model performance in clinical settings is highly sensitive to threshold selection; improving robustness to shifts between training and test distributions is a significant clinical and technical bottleneck.
