title: Generalizing forecasting outcome formats
---

**Background:** Current agentic forecasting benchmarks focus primarily on binary outcomes, which limits their applicability to real-world decision-making involving continuous or multi-category variables.

**Question / Future Work:** Generalizing agentic systems beyond binary probability estimation to support categorical outcomes and discretized cumulative distribution functions (CDFs) for continuous variables is an open challenge requiring new interface designs and coherence metrics.

**Why It Matters:** Moving toward multi-format forecasting is necessary for practical deployment in real-world policy and economic decision-making.
