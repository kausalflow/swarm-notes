title: Hierarchical extensions for HSGP-SSM
---

**Background:** Hierarchical state-space models allow for the pooling of information across related subjects to improve parameter estimation in dynamic systems.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop frameworks for integrating hierarchical GP models within Hilbert space approximations and associated sampling schemes to facilitate multilevel data analysis.

**Why It Matters:** Hierarchical extensions are essential for applied research where data are often collected from multiple individuals rather than a single source.

**Evidence:** However, it is currently unclear how these hierarchical GP models can be represented within the HSGP approximation and integrated in the Gibbs sampling scheme proposed in this paper.
