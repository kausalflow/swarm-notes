title: Inference for adaptively selected covariates
---

**Background:** In high-dimensional marginal regression settings, identifying a single most informative predictor among many candidates is a common task. Current methods for inference on adaptively selected indices in the presence of weak dependence often face significant theoretical and computational complexity.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop a formal theoretical framework and valid inferential procedures for the endogenous selection of an optimal covariate index in high-dimensional, weakly dependent time series settings.

**Why It Matters:** Formalizing inference for endogenously selected predictors is critical for the reliability of predictive modeling in high-dimensional regimes.
