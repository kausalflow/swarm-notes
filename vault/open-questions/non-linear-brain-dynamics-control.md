title: Non-linear brain dynamics control
---

**Background:** Network control theory typically relies on linear dynamical systems, which may not accurately represent brain transitions between complex, non-linear states.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should investigate the extent to which the current edge-centric control framework generalizes when the brain moves between distinct dynamical regimes, given that current controllability metrics are based on linear approximations.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a fundamental limitation in the applicability of network control theory to the non-linear, multi-state nature of human brain dynamics.
