title: Optimizing Ellipsoidal Decomposition Parameters
---

**Background:** The ellipsoidal decomposition framework decomposes target distributions into infinite mixtures of component distributions on nested compact sets, necessitating efficient methods for exact sampling.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the optimal construction of nested ellipsoidal sets (radii, count) to maximize the minorization constant and minimize coalescence time, particularly as dimensionality increases, remains a key challenge for scalable posterior sampling.

**Why It Matters:** Optimization of these parameters is essential for the practical viability and scalability of the proposed posterior sampling framework.
