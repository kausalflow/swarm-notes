title: Integrating Physical Dynamics in Forecasting
---

**Background:** PM2.5 forecasting is often limited by a reliance on statistical correlations that fail to capture the underlying physical complexity of pollutant dispersion and transformation.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work must address how to effectively incorporate complex physical processes such as boundary-layer dynamics, vertical mixing, and chemical formation into data-driven spatio-temporal models without sacrificing the computational efficiency of modern neural architectures.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between empirical spatiotemporal modeling and formal chemical-transport simulations is necessary to improve predictive reliability during extreme pollution events.
