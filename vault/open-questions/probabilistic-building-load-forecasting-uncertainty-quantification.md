title: Probabilistic Building Load Forecasting
---

**Background:** Building load forecasting is currently primarily focused on deterministic point predictions, which provide specific values without explicitly characterizing the associated statistical confidence.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending the deterministic forecasting framework to a probabilistic approach is essential for quantifying predictive uncertainty, which can significantly enhance decision-making reliability in grid operations and demand response management.

**Why It Matters:** Probabilistic forecasting is a major requirement for robust power system operations that deterministic models do not fulfill.
