title: Principled TSFM Data Curation
---

**Background:** Data curation practices for time series foundation models have historically relied on ad-hoc approaches, such as manual selection of mixtures or empirical hyperparameter sweeps, rather than principled strategies. This contrasts with the highly developed methodologies found in natural language processing.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to develop a principled framework for time series data curation, including systematic approaches for quality filtering, deduplication, annotation, and curriculum design, moving beyond empirical trial-and-error.

**Why It Matters:** Standardized data curation is a critical bottleneck for the predictable scaling of time series foundation models.

**Evidence:** Data curation in TSFMs has been ad hoc. In language modeling, data curation is treated as a first-class research problem... TSFM research has not gotten there yet.
