title: Assimilate live observations
---

**Background:** The current model requires a separate data-assimilation step or encoder to process live, potentially heterogeneous, observational data streams.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should focus on the assimilation of live observations directly into the model framework to improve forecast skill, moving beyond reliance solely on pre-processed reanalysis datasets.
