title: Fisher Information in QRC
---

**Background:** Adaptive phase estimation experiments using reconfigurable integrated photonic circuits have demonstrated that indistinguishable photon states exhibit higher Fisher information, leading to increased sensitivity to input parameters.

**Question / Future Work:** The observed performance enhancement in photonic quantum reservoir computing when using indistinguishable two-photon states could be formally linked to their higher Fisher information, which makes the output probabilities more responsive to changes in encoded input data. Investigating the precise relationship between this information-theoretic metric and the improved expressivity of the reservoir remains a key open research direction.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing a theoretical link between quantum-enhanced sensing metrics (Fisher information) and the performance of quantum machine learning models would provide a foundational basis for optimizing the design of such systems.

**Evidence:** Such increased sensitivity can be interpreted in terms of higher Fisher Information associated with indistinguishable two-photon states... and investigating this connection further represents a promising direction for future research.
