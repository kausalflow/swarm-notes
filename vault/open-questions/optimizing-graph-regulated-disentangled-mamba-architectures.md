title: Optimizing Graph-Regulated Disentangled Mamba

**Background:** Remote sensing time series frequently suffer from complex coupling between spatial, spectral, and temporal information dimensions.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the optimal strategy for balancing topological graph regulation with disentangled state-space model architectures remains a challenge for scaling environmental classification to heterogeneous global datasets.

**Why It Matters:** This question identifies a critical bottleneck in applying state-space models to multi-dimensional remote sensing tasks, where feature disentanglement is essential for generalization.
