title: Volatility-Aware Scenario Reweighting
---

**Background:** Dynamic scenario reweighting and adaptive trading strategies rely on current market information, but many current frameworks for intraday price forecasting do not explicitly calibrate reweighting mechanisms to evolving volatility.

**Question / Future Work:** Explore whether extending scenario reweighting mechanisms to explicitly account for volatility-sensitive weightings improves the robustness of trading strategies during periods of high market turbulence and regime shifts.

**Why It Matters:** Explicitly integrating volatility dynamics into the update process is essential for risk management and performance stability in high-frequency financial markets.
