title: Automated Selection of Biarchetype Parameters
---

**Background:** Biarchetype analysis identifies extreme, representative patterns across both observations and variables or temporal dimensions, typically requiring the specification of the number of archetypes.

**Question / Future Work:** Current selection methods for the number of row and column archetypes in biarchetype analysis rely on subjective visual inspection of RSS surfaces, lacking robust statistical criteria or automated validation procedures. Establishing objective, formal criteria is critical for the reproducible and scalable application of this framework.

**Why It Matters:** The choice of hyperparameters dictates model complexity and interpretability, making automated selection essential for preventing overfitting and ensuring consistent analysis.
