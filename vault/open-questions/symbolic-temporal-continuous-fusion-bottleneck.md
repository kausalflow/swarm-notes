title: Symbolic-Temporal-Continuous Fusion Bottleneck
---

**Background:** Symbolic representation of time series discretizes continuous signals to capture structural patterns like turning points. However, reconciling these discrete structures with continuous sequential dynamics and macroscopic domain semantics remains a significant modeling bottleneck.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate more robust fusion mechanisms for reconciling discrete symbolic representations with continuous temporal dynamics and external textual guidance to overcome shape smoothing in non-stationary forecasting. This involves moving beyond simple concatenation towards architectures that explicitly align discrete event-based tokens with global trend-steering mechanisms.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing the trade-off between local shape fidelity (captured by symbolic tokens) and global trend adherence (steered by textual semantics) is central to advancing multimodal time series forecasting.
