title: Extending geometric realignment to 3D
---

**Background:** Mobile robot navigation in 3D environments often involves unstructured terrains that go beyond simple 2D kinematic assumptions.

**Question / Future Work:** The current approach relies on SE(2) transformations to re-align temporal lag into spatial offsets. Future work needs to extend this analytical geometric re-projection mechanism to accommodate complex 3D unstructured environments, where roll, pitch, and elevation changes cannot be captured by 2D transformations alone.

**Why It Matters:** Generalizing to 3D is critical for deploying mobile navigation in diverse, non-planar real-world environments such as uneven outdoor terrain or multi-level indoor spaces.

**Evidence:** Future work will explore extending this analytical geometric re-projection mechanism to more complex 3D unstructured environments
