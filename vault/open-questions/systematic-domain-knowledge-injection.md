title: Systematic Domain Knowledge Injection
---

**Background:** Autonomous ML engineering aims to discover optimal model architectures and training procedures for specific tasks.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing methods to systematically inject domain-specific knowledge, such as physical laws, seasonal priors, or operational constraints, into the search process through specialized prompt templates or constrained code generation, will allow the agent to start from a stronger knowledge foundation.
