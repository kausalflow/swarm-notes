title: Optimizing TSFM Domain Fine-Tuning
---

**Background:** Domain-specific fine-tuning of Time Series Foundation Models involves training models on combined datasets to improve performance on unseen domains, yet the effectiveness of different fine-tuning strategies remains inconclusive.

**Question / Future Work:** Further research is required to determine effective fine-tuning strategies for TSFMs. Current approaches often yield only marginal improvements, highlighting the need for more sophisticated methodologies such as balanced data strategies, selective parameter tuning, or architecture-aware adaptation.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing robust, reliable fine-tuning recipes is essential to bridge the gap between zero-shot foundation model performance and task-specific accuracy in sensitive infrastructure domains.
