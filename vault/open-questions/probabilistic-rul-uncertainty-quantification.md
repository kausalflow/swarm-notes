title: Probabilistic RUL Uncertainty Quantification
---

**Background:** Remaining Useful Life (RUL) estimation often uses foundation models for feature extraction, but these models typically output point estimates rather than probabilistic distributions.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to leverage the probabilistic outputs of pretrained time-series foundation models (such as Chronos-2) to provide calibrated uncertainty estimates, which are essential for informing high-stakes industrial maintenance decisions.

**Why It Matters:** Uncertainty quantification is critical for the safety and reliability of predictive maintenance systems, allowing operators to better manage risks associated with potential equipment failures.
