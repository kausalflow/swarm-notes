title: Adaptive Gate Regularization in KANs
---

**Background:** Gated Residual Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KANs) use gating mechanisms to suppress nonlinear KAN components when simple linear models are sufficient.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing mechanisms for adaptive, dataset-specific gate regularization that automatically balances sparsity pressure with nonlinear correction requirements is critical for scaling gated KAN architectures to heterogeneous time-series data streams.

**Why It Matters:** Automating the regularization of gating mechanisms is essential for the practical deployment of gated architectures across diverse, unseen data regimes without tedious per-dataset hyperparameter tuning.
