title: Temporal Dynamics of Tail Parameters
---

**Background:** Probabilistic time series models often treat tail parameters as static or independently predicted snapshots, failing to account for their intricate, time-varying nature in non-stationary environments.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should investigate explicitly modeling the temporal dependencies of Lévy stability parameters as latent processes, potentially through dedicated state-space components, to better capture transitions in tail heaviness and asymmetry.

**Why It Matters:** Explicit modeling of tail dynamics is essential for detecting regime shifts from stable conditions to extreme volatility, which is crucial for dynamic risk management.
