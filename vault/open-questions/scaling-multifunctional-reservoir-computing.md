title: Scaling multifunctional reservoir computing
---

**Background:** Reservoir computing (RC) architectures often struggle to maintain predictive accuracy and reliable retrieval when tasked with emulating multiple distinct chaotic systems. As the number of target systems increases, the interference between dynamical representations typically degrades model performance.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematic investigation is needed to quantify the scaling limits of multifunctional reservoir computing, specifically how increased system diversity impacts the reconstruction quality of bifurcation diagrams and long-term statistical properties.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling to handle multiple distinct attractors is a fundamental bottleneck for deploying resource-constrained neuromorphic hardware in complex dynamical environments.
