title: Multivariate TSC GP Extension
---

**Background:** Time series classification often involves multivariate data where relationships between variables are essential for accurate classification. Existing feature learning methods in genetic programming currently focus primarily on univariate time series.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing extensions to handle multivariate time series data is a critical area for research, involving the design of program structures capable of capturing cross-variate dependencies.

**Why It Matters:** Multivariate TSC is prevalent in real-world applications, and current GP approaches are limited by their univariate focus.
