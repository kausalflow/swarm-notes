title: Personalization for Gesture Recognition
---

**Background:** Wrist-worn gesture recognition systems currently lack established personalization or user adaptation strategies, despite significant inter-subject variability in gesture execution.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate efficient personalization techniques to enhance the robustness of gesture recognition systems beyond standard subject-independent benchmarks, particularly under strict edge privacy constraints.

**Why It Matters:** Personalization is a critical bottleneck for making gesture recognition functional across diverse populations and reducing per-user calibration requirements.
