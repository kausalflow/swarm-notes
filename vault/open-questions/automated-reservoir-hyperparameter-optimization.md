title: Automated Reservoir Hyperparameter Optimization
---

**Background:** Reservoir computing models typically rely on fixed recurrent dynamics that require extensive manual hyperparameter tuning to ensure stability and predictive performance.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing systematic, automated procedures for optimizing internal hyperparameters (such as input/bias scaling and spectral radius) is necessary to close the performance gap with gradient-based models and enable broader, scalable deployment.

**Why It Matters:** The dependence on manual hyperparameter tuning limits the adoption and robustness of reservoir computing in diverse or non-stationary application domains.
