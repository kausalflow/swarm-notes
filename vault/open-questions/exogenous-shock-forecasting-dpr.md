title: Integrating Exogenous Covariates in DPR
---

**Background:** Deep time series forecasting models typically rely on endogenous history, making them reactive to external events.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is required to extend token-level adaptation mechanisms like DPR to incorporate multimodal exogenous covariates for anticipatory recalibration in the face of external shocks.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing the limitation of endogenous-only prediction is critical for robust forecasting in real-world environments subject to external perturbations.

**Evidence:** DPR relies exclusively on endogenous temporal features, leaving exogenous shocks undetected until they manifest in the signal. Future work will integrate multimodal covariates... for anticipatory recalibration.
