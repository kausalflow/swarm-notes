title: Clinical intervention simulation fidelity
---

**Background:** Generative world models for physiology aim to simulate individual patient responses to clinical interventions in silico.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the systemic limitations and error propagation characteristics when using generative health world models to simulate complex, long-term intervention effects across diverse physiological domains, compared to traditional clinical trial evidence.

**Why It Matters:** Validating the causal fidelity and reliability of in silico intervention simulation is critical before such models can be safely integrated into clinical decision-support workflows.
