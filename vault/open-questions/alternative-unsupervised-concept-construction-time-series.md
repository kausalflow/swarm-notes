title: Alternative and Unsupervised Concept Construction
---

**Background:** Concept-based counterfactual generation for time series relies on predefined feature extraction and decomposition methods.

**Question / Future Work:** Explore alternative types of concepts, such as localized patterns to unify concept-based methods with subsequence-based approaches, autocorrelation, or unsupervised concept construction techniques, ensuring that concepts remain modifiable in a human-interpretable manner.

**Why It Matters:** Expanding the definition and construction of concepts beyond rigid global transformations can significantly broaden the applicability and expressiveness of concept-based explainability methods in complex temporal domains.

**Evidence:** Furthermore, in future research, other types of concepts could be considered. For example, localized patterns, thereby unifying ConceptCF with subsequence-based approaches, or autocorrelation. Additionally, unsupervised concept construction could be explored. Important is that concepts can be modified in an interpretable manner.
