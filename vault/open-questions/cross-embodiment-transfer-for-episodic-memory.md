**Background:** Research on embodied intelligence and robotic manipulation systems often identifies limitations concerning generalization beyond the training environment.

**Question / Future Work:** The current experimental evaluation uses a single robot platform (UR5e), leaving the challenge of cross-embodiment transfer of the learned memory and control system as a future area of investigation.
