title: VLM Visual Resolution Bottlenecks
---

**Background:** Vision-Language Models (VLMs) serve as visual judges for forecasting, yet this paradigm is currently limited by the resolution of rendered plots and the visual acuity of existing models. For high-frequency or long-horizon series, visual compression can obscure critical geometric features, leading to degradation in judgment quality.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematically investigating how to overcome the information loss associated with visual rendering and plotting, and developing specialized VLMs or visualization techniques capable of preserving fine-grained structural features in long-duration or complex time series for more reliable VLM-based judgment.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental bottleneck for the reliability and scalability of visual-based forecasting evaluation.
