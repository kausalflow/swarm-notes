title: Scaling Fidelity for Underrepresented Populations
---

**Background:** Simulation fidelity in LLMs often varies significantly by population, showing lower performance for groups underrepresented in web-scale pre-training data.

**Question / Future Work:** It is currently unclear how to develop robust scaling strategies that close the simulation fidelity gap for populations underrepresented in existing pre-training corpora. Future research is required to determine whether this disparity can be mitigated through data curation, adaptive fine-tuning, or if it represents an inherent limitation that requires new methodological approaches.

**Why It Matters:** Critical for ensuring that social simulations do not exacerbate existing societal biases or representational gaps.
