title: Principled dissimilarity selection framework
---

**Background:** Euclidean mirrors leverage dissimilarity measures to retain signal about network dynamics from latent position processes. The informativeness of these mirrors is highly dependent on the choice of the dissimilarity measure.

**Question / Future Work:** Establish a formal framework for choosing optimal dissimilarity measures in mirror construction that is specifically tailored to the underlying class of latent position processes.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing a principled selection criterion is necessary for the consistent and robust application of mirrors in dynamic network inference.

**Evidence:** how does one choose a dissimilarity in a principled way to best extract information about the dynamics within a class of LPPs?
