title: Autonomous Shift Detection Adaptation
---

**Background:** Continual learning systems are often deployed in environments where task boundaries are not known a priori, unlike laboratory settings where they are signaled.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to develop mechanisms that can autonomously detect and adapt to distribution shifts in online streaming scenarios without relying on explicit task boundary signals.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental prerequisite for moving continual learning from controlled incremental learning tasks to real-world, unconstrained deployment.

**Evidence:** Evaluating the framework in truly online scenarios where class boundaries are not explicitly signalled, and where the model must detect and adapt to distribution shifts autonomously, would bring the system closer to real-world deployment conditions.
