title: Conditioning on covariates
---

**Background:** Mortality forecasting models can be improved by incorporating external socioeconomic or health-related variables that influence mortality rates alongside the structural dynamics captured by the model.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should investigate conditioning the flow-field dynamics on external covariates such as GDP per capita or health expenditure to potentially enhance forecast accuracy and mechanistic understanding.
