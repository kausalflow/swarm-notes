title: Incorporate physics-informed losses
---

**Background:** Data-driven weather models may not fully incorporate the known laws governing atmospheric and oceanic dynamics.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should explore the incorporation of physics-informed loss functions during training to embed fundamental physical principles into the learned atmospheric dynamics, potentially improving generalization and forecast fidelity beyond what purely data-driven objectives achieve.
