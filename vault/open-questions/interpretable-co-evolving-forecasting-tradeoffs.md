title: Interpretable Co-evolving Forecasting Tradeoffs
---

**Background:** Large language models are increasingly adapted for time series forecasting, but effectively capturing and reasoning over complex, non-stationary temporal dependencies in co-evolving datasets remains a challenge.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research must determine the extent to which structural serialization of dependency graphs can achieve competitive accuracy against state-of-the-art specialized models without sacrificing transparency, particularly as systems scale to larger numbers of co-evolving variables.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses the fundamental tension between model transparency and predictive performance in high-stakes time-series applications.
