title: CI vs CD Forecasting Performance
---

**Background:** Multivariate time series forecasting often involves choosing between Channel-Independent (CI) strategies, which treat channels independently, and Channel-Dependent (CD) strategies, which explicitly model inter-channel correlations.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the specific dataset characteristics and regime factors that determine whether Channel-Independent or Channel-Dependent strategies provide superior forecasting accuracy when using tabular foundation models.

**Why It Matters:** This addresses a fundamental trade-off in multivariate forecasting that remains poorly understood as foundation model architectures become more prevalent.

**Evidence:** Determining precisely when a CI or CD method is superior remains an open question in the field.
