title: Multimodal Physiological Input Integration
---

**Background:** Clinical diagnostics often involve integrating diverse physiological data streams to provide comprehensive assessments of health states.

**Question / Future Work:** Methods are needed to extend transformer architectures to perform efficient, synchronized fusion of asynchronous, multimodal physiological sensor data under strict latency and power constraints.

**Why It Matters:** Unimodal sensors lack sufficient context to distinguish between complex, overlapping physiological states, which is essential for reliable closed-loop medical control.
