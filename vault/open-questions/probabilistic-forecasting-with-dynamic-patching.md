title: Probabilistic Forecasting Integration
---

**Background:** Probabilistic forecasting is a desirable capability in time series analysis, extending point predictions to include uncertainty estimates.

**Question / Future Work:** The authors mention that probabilistic forecasts can be easily provided by substituting the model’s linear projection head with a probabilistic head. Further development in integrating the dynamic patching mechanism with specialized probabilistic output heads to assess its impact on uncertainty estimation is a potential area for future exploration.
