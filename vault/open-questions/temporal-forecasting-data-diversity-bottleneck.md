title: Temporal forecasting data diversity
---

**Background:** Evaluation of language models on resolved forecasting questions often suffers from data leakage, necessitating protocols that strictly constrain the information available to the model.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is needed to expand the diversity and density of temporally-pinned corpora beyond narrow community-driven social media to support robust, general-purpose forecasting evaluations.

**Why It Matters:** The reliance on narrow data sources creates systemic evaluation bias, obscuring model performance on topics not well-represented in existing corpora.

**Evidence:** The archive is built from a fixed set of 20 public subreddits, so the corpus skews English-language and community-driven.
