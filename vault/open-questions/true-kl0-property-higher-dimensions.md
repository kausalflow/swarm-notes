title: Higher-Dimensional True-KL0 Limits
---

**Background:** Scored elicitation mechanisms for AI oversight depend on the dimensionality of agent report spaces to maintain incentive compatibility.

**Question / Future Work:** While the True-KL0 property holds for d ≤ 4, the behavior of incentive-compatible scored elicitation mechanisms in d ≥ 6 remains an open research area. Determining whether fundamental limits exist or if alternative mechanism designs can extend these guarantees to higher-dimensional spaces is crucial for robust AI oversight.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling AI oversight to more complex task environments requires understanding how mechanism design properties degrade with dimensionality.
