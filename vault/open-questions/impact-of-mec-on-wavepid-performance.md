title: MEC Impact on Flavor Identification
---

**Background:** Neutrino interaction models used in detector simulations often exclude Meson Exchange Current (MEC) processes, which significantly influence the energy partitioning between outgoing particles in neutrino events.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematically quantify the impact of Meson Exchange Current (MEC) interactions on the performance of timing-based classifiers like WavePID, especially in the low-energy regime where energy partitioning strongly influences Cherenkov emission geometry.

**Why It Matters:** MEC affects light-yield characteristics crucial for flavor identification; understanding this impact is essential for classifier robustness in next-generation experiments.

**Evidence:** We leave detailed evaluation of the impact of MEC on WavePID to future work.
