title: Scalability to High-Dimensional Systems
---

**Background:** Machine learning models often rely on large historical datasets to forecast dynamical systems, limiting their applicability in data-constrained scenarios. Extending meta-learning approaches from low-dimensional synthetic models to high-dimensional, complex, real-world systems remains a significant research bottleneck.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate methods to scale meta-learning frameworks to high-dimensional dynamical systems, potentially through the integration of unsupervised feature extraction like autoencoders to capture meaningful latent representations.

**Why It Matters:** This bottleneck prevents the application of advanced meta-learning forecasters to complex, real-world climate or biological datasets.
