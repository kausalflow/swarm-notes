title: Collision BH Formation Validation
---

**Background:** The formation of massive seed black holes via stellar collisions in dense star clusters is predicted to occur on timescales dependent on cluster size and mass.

**Question / Future Work:** The assumption that stellar evolution effects (like mass loss and subsequent cluster expansion) do not strongly alter the derived relationship between black hole formation efficiency, cluster mass, and critical mass needs validation through more detailed stellar dynamical simulations for this regime.
