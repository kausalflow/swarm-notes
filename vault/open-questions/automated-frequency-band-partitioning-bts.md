title: Automated Frequency Band Partitioning
---

**Background:** Biomedical time-series signals often contain consistent oscillatory patterns that are obscured by subject-dependent variations in spectral amplitude and phase.

**Question / Future Work:** The efficacy of frequency-domain alignment mechanisms is heavily dependent on the chosen frequency segmentation, yet current approaches often rely on manual or fixed heuristic partitions. Developing data-driven strategies to automate frequency-band selection that captures task-relevant physiological rhythms is a key challenge for scalable cross-subject generalization.

**Why It Matters:** This question highlights a critical bottleneck in deploying spectral alignment models across diverse physiological datasets where optimal frequency bands are unknown.
