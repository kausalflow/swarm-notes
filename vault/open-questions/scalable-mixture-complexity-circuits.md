title: Scalable mixture component scaling
---

**Background:** The computational complexity of probabilistic circuits in multivariate forecasting often scales cubically with the number of mixture components due to the aggregation requirements in the circuit structure.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing methods to decouple the expressivity of the model, which is tied to the number of mixture components, from the cubic computational overhead is a critical research direction for scaling these models to high-dimensional datasets.

**Why It Matters:** Overcoming this scalability bottleneck is essential for applying circuit-based probabilistic models to real-world multivariate scenarios with complex dependency structures.
