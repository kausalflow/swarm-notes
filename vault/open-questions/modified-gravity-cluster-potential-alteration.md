title: Investigate Modified Gravity Potentials
---

**Background:** The gravitational redshift signal is generally modeled assuming a one-component, spherically symmetric Navarro-Frenk-White (NFW) total mass profile for galaxy clusters.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work should be devoted to investigating how more modified gravity models surviving the GW170817 detection alter the gravitational potentials of clusters.
