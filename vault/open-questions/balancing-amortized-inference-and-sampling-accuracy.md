title: Balancing Amortization and Sampling Accuracy
---

**Background:** Normalizing flow-based models provide amortized Bayesian inference for gravitational-wave signals, yet their parameter precision in high-dimensional or highly correlated regions often falls short of iterative sampling methods.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate hybrid techniques that combine the speed of amortized flow-based inference with the accuracy of iterative MCMC sampling to capture fine-grained posterior structures without compromising latency requirements.

**Why It Matters:** Bridging the gap between speed and exactness is critical for high-stakes, low-latency scientific discovery where parameter errors have direct consequences for follow-up observation strategies.
