title: Embedded Implementation and Certification
---

**Background:** Autonomous navigation for planetary landing requires high-fidelity diagnostics that are computationally efficient enough for onboard flight hardware. The proposed framework demonstrates potential for early detection but requires further validation for real-time deployment.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to transition the proposed fusion framework into a certified, real-time onboard implementation, including optimizing the algebraic co-state updates for flight processors and establishing formal theoretical guarantees to support operational certification.

**Why It Matters:** Moving from experimental success on telemetry data to flight-ready software requires addressing strict computational and formal certification bottlenecks that remain unresolved.

**Evidence:** Systematic validation across diverse missions and failure modes, including ROC-based detection analysis, uncertainty quantification of generator parameters and MFPT, and robustness studies... are essential for operational readiness.
