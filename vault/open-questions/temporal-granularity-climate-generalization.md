title: Generalization to Diverse Temporal Granularities
---

**Background:** Time series foundation models can be adapted to regional climate forecasting without modifying backbone parameters using lightweight inference-time wrappers.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematically investigate how inference-time black-box wrappers generalize across diverse temporal granularities, broader climate regimes, and alternative hydrological targets beyond monthly drought indices.

**Why It Matters:** Determines the scope, limits, and environmental generalizability of zero-shot black-box adaptation frameworks for time series foundation models.

**Evidence:** Future work needs to systematically validate the generalization and robustness of multi-resolution temporal wrappers across substantially different temporal granularities and climate regimes.
