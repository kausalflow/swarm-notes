title: Demographic Data Availability Gaps
---

**Background:** State-level demographic data for forensic DNA databases in the United States is currently limited to a small subset of jurisdictions.

**Question / Future Work:** Comprehensive demographic representation (sex and race) across all 50 states remains unavailable, as current data is restricted to seven states that responded to Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests. Establishing a national, longitudinal understanding of the demographic composition of forensic DNA databases requires more widespread and consistent reporting from state agencies.

**Why It Matters:** Identifying demographic biases and disparities within forensic DNA databases is critical for equitable policy development and understanding the impact of criminal justice interventions, yet the lack of standardized national data hinders this research.
