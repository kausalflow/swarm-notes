title: Online Adaptation to Topology Drift
---

**Background:** Microservice systems frequently undergo dynamic structural changes, such as service insertion, removal, or routing policy updates, which alter the underlying call paths.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigating robust mechanisms for predictive models to adapt to persistent topology drift or to automatically trigger retraining when the assumed call graph becomes invalid is a key bottleneck for production deployment.

**Why It Matters:** Topology drift is pervasive in cloud-native environments, and static graph assumptions limit the long-term utility of trace-based forecasting models.
