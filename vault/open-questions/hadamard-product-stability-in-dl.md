title: Hadamard product stability in DL
---

**Background:** The Hadamard product of matrices is frequently used as a fundamental operation in various deep learning architectures.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the utility of theoretical eigenvalue lower bounds for Hadamard products in assessing the stability and conditioning of neural network layers or optimization objectives that rely on element-wise matrix multiplication.

**Why It Matters:** Establishing theoretical guarantees for the conditioning of Hadamard-based operations can lead to more robust model training and architectural design.
