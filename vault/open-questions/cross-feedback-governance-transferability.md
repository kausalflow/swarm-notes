title: Generalizing Insight Governance Across Feedback
---

**Background:** LLM agents interacting with non-stationary environments often struggle to retain useful insights while discarding stale ones, creating a retention-forgetting dilemma.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to determine if governance structures for verbal rules transfer effectively across heterogeneous types of world feedback (e.g., immediate vs. delayed signals) to ensure robust performance in diverse non-stationary environments.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for establishing if non-parametric agentic governance is a generalizable architecture or limited to specific domains like financial forecasting.

**Evidence:** Whether the same evidence structures and governance patterns transfer across feedback types remains open.
