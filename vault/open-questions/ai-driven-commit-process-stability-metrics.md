title: AI-driven stability metric validity
---

**Background:** Modern software development increasingly relies on Large Language Model (LLM) tools to generate, refactor, and document code.

**Question / Future Work:** Research is needed to determine if the fractal scaling exponent alpha, identified as a marker of software process stability in human-driven development, remains a valid or interpretable indicator when commit processes are driven primarily by generative AI tools, which may introduce different temporal correlation patterns.

**Why It Matters:** Critical for ensuring software engineering metrics remain relevant as autonomous agentic coding and LLM-based development cycles become ubiquitous.
