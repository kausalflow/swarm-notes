title: Reliable Structural Damage Quantification
---

**Background:** Damage quantification remains a challenging aspect of vibration-based structural health monitoring, frequently involving reliance on scalar metrics derived from learned latent representations.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research is required to develop more reliable damage scoring methods and to explore the integration of physics-informed indicators with learned latent representations to improve the accuracy and reliability of structural damage quantification.

**Why It Matters:** Reliable damage quantification is essential for informed decision-making regarding structural maintenance and service life extension, moving beyond simple detection towards actionable condition assessment.
