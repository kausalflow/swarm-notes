title: Application to Dynamic Domains
---

**Background:** The current framework synthesizes causal, local, and periodic inductive biases for financial time series forecasting using a Transformer backbone.

**Question / Future Work:** The TIPS framework should be applied to other dynamic, non-stationary domains, such as energy load forecasting, traffic flow prediction, and climate modeling, to test the generalizability of regime-dependent inductive bias synthesis.
