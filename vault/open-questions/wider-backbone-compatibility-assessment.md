title: Wider Backbone Compatibility
---

**Background:** The proposed framework recursively splits IMTS samples based on time periods to maintain original sampling patterns across multiple scales. The method's compatibility was demonstrated across various IMTS backbones.

**Question / Future Work:** Explore combining the recursive multi-scale approach with a wider range of existing IMTS backbone architectures beyond those tested, especially to assess the potential for boosting performance in models with fundamentally different internal mechanisms.
