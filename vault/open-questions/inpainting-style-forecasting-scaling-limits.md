title: Inpainting Forecasting Scaling Limits
---

**Background:** Generative diffusion models increasingly treat future time-series prediction as a masked completion task.

**Question / Future Work:** It remains unclear how the inpainting formulation scales across varying forecasting horizons and whether the masking strategy introduces systematic biases compared to standard regression or autoregressive approaches.

**Why It Matters:** Understanding the limitations of inpainting-style generation is critical for moving beyond empirical benchmarking and establishing theoretical bounds on generative time-series models.

**Evidence:** The results highlight the effectiveness of integrating diffusion-based generative modeling with an inpainting formulation for robust, flexible, and high-fidelity solar power forecasting.
