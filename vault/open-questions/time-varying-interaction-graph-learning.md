title: Time-Varying Interaction Graph Learning
---

**Background:** Many neural flow models for irregular multivariate time series assume static inter-variable relationships, which may not hold in non-stationary domains.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending flow-based interaction models to incorporate time-varying graph structures remains a substantial challenge for capturing evolving dependencies in real-world systems. Addressing this is crucial for the framework's applicability to tasks like forecasting and dynamic state estimation.

**Why It Matters:** The current assumption of time-invariant interaction graphs limits model flexibility for non-stationary systems. Future work in this direction is essential for broader applicability.
