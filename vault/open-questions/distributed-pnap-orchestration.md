title: Distributed PNap Orchestration
---

**Background:** In Multi-access Edge Computing (MEC), managing the sleep states of edge servers (ECs) and the lifecycle of hosted services must be jointly considered to balance energy consumption and service availability.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing a distributed version of the PowerNap (PNap) framework, capable of scaling efficiently without the prohibitive computational and signaling overhead of a centralized orchestrator as the number of users, ECs, and services grows.
