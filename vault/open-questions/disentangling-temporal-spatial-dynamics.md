title: Disentangling Spatio-Temporal Dynamics
---

**Background:** Spatio-temporal forecasting often relies on deep learning to model complex dependencies in non-stationary data, yet current models struggle to disentangle heterogeneous temporal signals from dynamic, sparse spatial interactions.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing methods that effectively disentangle mixed temporal patterns and capture time-varying, sparse spatial dependencies in large-scale road networks remains an open challenge. Future work must refine adaptive decomposition techniques and dynamic masking mechanisms to better handle high-dimensional, non-stationary data without introducing computational bottlenecks.

**Why It Matters:** Crucial for improving robustness and interpretability in traffic forecasting, as current models often compress diverse patterns into shared representations or rely on noise-amplifying dense attention.
