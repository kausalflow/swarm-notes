title: Stablecoin Downside Volatility Causality Gap
---

**Background:** Analysis of stablecoin market factors and cryptocurrency market factors across various time horizons has revealed inconsistencies between in-sample causality and out-of-sample predictive power.

**Question / Future Work:** The study observed statistically significant causality running from stablecoin downside volatility to cryptocurrency downside volatility across daily, weekly, and monthly horizons, yet this signal resulted in a marginal Mean Squared Error (MSE) reduction in out-of-sample forecasts. Future work should investigate the causal role of stablecoin downside volatility on cryptocurrency market movements, particularly addressing why the observed long-term causal link does not translate to strong short-term predictive accuracy in daily forecasting models.
