title: Foresight-Driven Retrieval Robustness
---

**Background:** Current research agents often fail to effectively leverage external context for time-series forecasting, frequently being misled by distractor information.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the design of foresight-driven retrieval architectures that can robustly identify, distill, and prioritize relevant evidence for time-series forecasting in the presence of high distractor noise.

**Why It Matters:** This is a fundamental challenge for the development of autonomous agents capable of performing reliable, context-aware forecasting in real-world scenarios.

**Evidence:** Our results motivate research on foresight-driven agents that search for the right context to predict the future.
