title: Multi-channel dynamical system discovery
---

**Background:** Many real-world systems are characterized by multi-channel dynamics where states are composed of coupled internal variables.

**Question / Future Work:** Generalizing symbolic identification to multi-channel message passing and cross-variable basis discovery is essential for accurately reconstructing systems with complex internal state dependencies.

**Why It Matters:** Standard symbolic identification often assumes simple state representations; extending to multi-channel systems is required for high-fidelity modeling.

**Evidence:** First, systems with coupled internal states may benefit from multi-channel message passing and cross-variable bases.
