title: Clinical Foundation Model Generalization
---

**Background:** Large-scale clinical foundation models ingest highly complex, heterogeneous, and longitudinal data to generate unified embeddings for downstream forecasting.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the extent to which latent representations derived from large-scale, institution-specific electronic health record systems generalize across different hospital environments, demographics, and clinical practices.

**Why It Matters:** Generalization is the primary hurdle for the clinical deployment of foundation models; understanding cross-institutional representation drift is critical for 'computable medicine'.
