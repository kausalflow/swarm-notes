title: Cooperative Multi-Agent DF-RL Scaling

**Background:** Decision-focused learning integrates forecasting and downstream decision-making by optimizing forecaster parameters based on the impact of predictions on task-specific performance.

**Question / Future Work:** Extending decision-focused reinforcement learning (DF-RL) frameworks to cooperative, multi-agent settings requires scaling to manage complex grid constraints and optimizing system-level performance across multiple concurrent charging sessions.

**Why It Matters:** Developing scalable, cooperative multi-agent control is essential for moving from single-session EV charging to practical, grid-wide smart charging deployments.
