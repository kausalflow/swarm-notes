title: Electricity Volatility Skewness Limitations
---

**Background:** Electricity spot prices often exhibit high right-skewness and frequent extreme volatility spikes during market distress, posing challenges for standard linear autoregressive models.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing more flexible modeling frameworks or proxy estimation techniques to better account for the conditional skewness of electricity volatility, particularly during extreme market events where standard autoregressive models exhibit systematic under-reaction, remains a critical unresolved challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Addressing this limitation is crucial for improving tail-risk estimation and risk premia forecasting in highly volatile markets.

**Evidence:** This indicates that more flexibility is needed in the regression (19) or potentially a better proxy for the conditional skewness.
