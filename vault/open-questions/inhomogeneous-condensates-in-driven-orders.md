title: Spatially Inhomogeneous Condensates
---

**Background:** The current theoretical framework restricts the analysis to spatially homogeneous configurations of the order parameters.

**Question / Future Work:** The analysis should be extended beyond spatially homogeneous Ansätze to capture the physics associated with spatially inhomogeneous condensates, which are not described by the current approach.
