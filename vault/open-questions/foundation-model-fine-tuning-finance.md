title: Foundation Model Fine-tuning Utility
---

**Background:** Foundation models for time-series forecasting have been released, but their efficacy across specific financial sub-domains such as Treasury yield curve prediction remains largely unproven compared to traditional econometric models.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to explore the role of fine-tuning for foundational time-series models like TimeGPT in the context of yield curve forecasting, as well as to determine if their zero-shot performance can be surpassed by specialized fine-tuning or hybrid approaches.

**Why It Matters:** Financial time-series, particularly yield curves, possess unique structural properties; evaluating whether foundation models can adapt through fine-tuning is crucial for determining their practical utility in bond market applications versus traditional ARIMA-based methods.
