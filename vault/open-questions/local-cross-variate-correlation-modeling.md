title: Local Cross-Variate Correlation Modeling
---

**Background:** Multivariate time series forecasting models frequently utilize multi-scale architectures to capture dependencies across different temporal ranges, but these models often struggle to explicitly model dependencies between variates at local scales.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing methods that explicitly incorporate local cross-variate correlations to further enhance the predictive performance of existing multi-scale architectures, while maintaining computational efficiency, remains a significant challenge.

**Why It Matters:** This highlights a bottleneck in existing patch-based forecasting methods, as effectively modeling local dependencies is crucial for high-dimensional time series forecasting.

**Evidence:** One key limitation is that cross-variate correlations are not explicitly explored at a local level.
