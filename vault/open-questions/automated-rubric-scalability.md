title: Automated Research Evaluation Rubrics
---

**Background:** Evaluation rubrics for AI research replication currently rely on manual expert design, which limits scalability for large-scale, autonomous research discovery pipelines.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing scalable, dense reward signals for automated research discovery beyond simple result matching is necessary to evaluate the structural and semantic fidelity of replicated models.

**Why It Matters:** Scaling automated research discovery requires robust, dense, and automated feedback signals that go beyond simple 'result matching' to evaluate structural and semantic fidelity.

**Evidence:** Future work will expand the utility of AgentObjective by incorporating the remaining dimensions of the tree-structured rubric into the replication and optimization cycle.
