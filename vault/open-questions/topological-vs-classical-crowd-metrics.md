title: Topological vs. Classical Metrics
---

**Background:** Persistent homology characterizes relational data structures to identify complex system patterns.

**Question / Future Work:** Systematic benchmarking is required to compare topological data analysis against established macroscopic crowd observables (like density and velocity fields) to determine if topological methods offer unique informational advantages in characterizing regimes and phase transitions in human movement.

**Why It Matters:** Determining the specific informational value-add of topological descriptors is essential for moving beyond empirical proofs-of-concept toward practical utility in crowd management and safety applications.

**Evidence:** In future work we want to better understand which information we gain by using structural methods over classic analysis.
