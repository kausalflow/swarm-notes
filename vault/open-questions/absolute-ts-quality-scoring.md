title: Absolute Time Series Quality Scoring
---

**Background:** Time series quality assessment currently relies on pairwise comparisons for relative quality, leaving the development of absolute quality scoring models and large-scale ranking techniques for time series data largely unexplored.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work is required to extend frameworks beyond pairwise quality comparison to absolute quality scoring or large-scale ranking scenarios, which would enable the assignment of meaningful scalar quality metrics across large datasets.

**Why It Matters:** This is a foundational bottleneck for applying automated quality assessment to massive, unorganized datasets where pairwise comparisons are computationally prohibitive.

**Evidence:** Extending the framework to absolute quality scoring or large-scale ranking scenarios is an interesting direction for future work.
