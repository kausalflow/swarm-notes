title: Intra-Day Stablecoin Downside Causality
---

**Background:** Research modeling the influence of stablecoin market factors on cryptocurrency market volatility using daily data has suggested a discrepancy between observed causality and predictive accuracy.

**Question / Future Work:** The investigation into the relationship between stablecoin downside volatility and cryptocurrency upside volatility found that the former failed to generalize effectively out-of-sample, suggesting a deteriorated performance in predictive models. It is hypothesized that this failure is due to the daily timescale, as instantaneous investor reactions (where stablecoin downside volatility immediately precedes cryptocurrency upside rallies) may only be captured on an intra-day timeframe. Future research should repeat the methodology using intra-day time frames to capture these more instantaneous relationships and potentially resolve the missing link between stablecoin downside volatility and cryptocurrency upside volatility.
