title: Automated Group Discovery Strategies
---

**Background:** Counterfactual explanations for high-dimensional time-series classification often rely on manual, domain-specific feature groupings to improve interpretability. Current frameworks lack generalized, automated methods for discovering or learning these semantic groupings from data.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a significant need to develop data-driven strategies for automated feature group discovery in counterfactual generation. Developing such methods would move the field away from manual domain-specific constraints toward more scalable and adaptable interpretability frameworks for complex multi-sensor data.

**Why It Matters:** Automating group discovery is essential for transitioning explainable AI frameworks from hand-crafted, study-specific domains to robust, automated tools suitable for varied sensor configurations and clinical settings.
