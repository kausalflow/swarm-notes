title: Spatial-multivariate electricity forecasting
---

**Background:** Electricity markets exhibit inherent spatial dependence due to the physical structure of power grids and inter-zonal flow constraints. Current multivariate electricity price forecasting models primarily focus on temporal cross-hour dependencies.

**Question / Future Work:** There is a need to extend multivariate frameworks to jointly model electricity prices across multiple geographical locations to explicitly incorporate the spatial dependencies intrinsic to power systems.

**Why It Matters:** Incorporating spatial dependence is essential for scaling forecasting models to multi-node power systems and for maintaining consistency between locational marginal prices.
