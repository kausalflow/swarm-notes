title: Dimension Determination in SSA
---

**Background:** Stationary Subspace Analysis (SSA) and its spatial variant (spSSA) decompose multivariate data into stationary and nonstationary latent components, requiring the prespecification of the subspace dimensions.

**Question / Future Work:** Determining the dimension of the nonstationary subspace in blind source separation remains a fundamental challenge without a universally accepted or automated selection criterion. Future research is required to move beyond data-dependent heuristic methods toward robust, theoretically-grounded estimation procedures.

**Why It Matters:** This is a bottleneck for the practical deployment of subspace methods as it impacts the accuracy and reliability of component separation.

**Evidence:** However, determining this dimension remains a fundamental challenge in SSA, for which no generally accepted solution currently exists.
