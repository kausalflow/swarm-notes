title: Multi-Modal Data Fusion

**Background:** Models for fMRI time series prediction rely on integrating spatial and temporal features derived from brain imaging data.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should explore fusing multi-modal data (beyond just fMRI) into the forecasting framework to potentially improve the understanding and prediction of brain dynamics.
