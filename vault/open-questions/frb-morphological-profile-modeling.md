title: FRB Morphological Profile Modeling
---

**Background:** Fast radio bursts (FRBs) exhibit diverse temporal structures, including complex morphological features like decaying tails and multi-component sub-bursts.

**Question / Future Work:** The research community faces a challenge in moving beyond standard single-Gaussian profile fitting towards more sophisticated morphological characterization of transient pulses that exhibit asymmetry, multi-component structure, or non-Gaussian tails. Developing robust, automated fitting frameworks for these profiles is essential for precise physical parameter extraction.

**Why It Matters:** Essential for extracting accurate physical parameters from high-resolution radio observations and distinguishing between different emission physics.
