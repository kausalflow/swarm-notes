title: Robust Non-Gaussian Streaming Modeling
---

**Background:** Probabilistic time-series models and streaming change-detection mechanisms often rely on linear Gaussian state space assumptions for computational tractability.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing robust streaming models that can handle heavy-tailed noise, extreme outliers, or non-Gaussian distributions without sacrificing computational efficiency is a fundamental research challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Fundamental limitation for real-world stream processing where data quality and noise profiles are often non-ideal.
