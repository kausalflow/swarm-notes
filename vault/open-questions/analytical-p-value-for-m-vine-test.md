title: Analytical P-Value Derivation
---

**Background:** The proposed method utilizes M-vine copulas to model high-dimensional dependence structures in a flexible, non-parametric manner for testing Granger causality in expectiles. The computational procedure relies on simulation (bootstrapping) to determine the p-value under the null hypothesis.

**Question / Future Work:** Develop analytical or direct critical values for the M-vine test statistic, moving beyond the computationally intensive simulation-based (bootstrap) method used to determine the p-value under the null hypothesis for multivariate expectile Granger causality.
