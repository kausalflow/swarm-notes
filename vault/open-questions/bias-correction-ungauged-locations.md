title: Bias correction at ungauged locations
---

**Background:** Current bias correction methods for storm surge forecasting typically require paired observational and modeled data at specific geographic locations, limiting their applicability to regions with gauge infrastructure.

**Question / Future Work:** Developing frameworks to assess and correct storm surge biases at ungauged locations, where observational data is unavailable, remains an unresolved challenge requiring the incorporation of physical constraints.

**Why It Matters:** Extending bias correction to ungauged locations is essential for comprehensive coastal flood risk management, as gauge networks are spatially sparse.
