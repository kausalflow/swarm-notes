title: Data assimilation for flood twins
---

**Background:** Digital twins for metropolitan flooding require data assimilation of sparse, real-time observations into fast hydrodynamic surrogates to enable accurate nowcasting and state updating.

**Question / Future Work:** The integration of observed data into latent-space surrogates remains an unresolved bottleneck for operationalizing flood digital twins, particularly regarding the optimal assimilation cadence and the design of observation operators for meshless coordinate-based decoders.

**Why It Matters:** This is critical for transitioning from offline hydrodynamic emulation to an operational, observation-coupled digital twin.
