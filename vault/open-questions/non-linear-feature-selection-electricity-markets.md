title: Non-linear feature selection methods
---

**Background:** Intraday electricity market forecasting often utilizes features selected through linear methods.

**Question / Future Work:** Non-linear feature selection techniques may be necessary to capture complex, non-linear relationships and interactions among high-dimensional orderbook features that current linear selection methods potentially ignore.

**Why It Matters:** Linear feature selection can discard features whose predictive utility is purely non-linear, thereby limiting model performance.
