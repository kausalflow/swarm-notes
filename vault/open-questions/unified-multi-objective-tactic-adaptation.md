title: Unified Multi-Objective Tactic Adaptation
---

**Background:** Generative models for multi-agent coordination rely on guidance mechanisms to steer outputs towards strategic objectives. Existing approaches typically integrate these objectives through separate, modular constraints such as rules or pre-trained models.

**Question / Future Work:** The development of a unified multi-objective guidance framework that can dynamically balance conflicting strategic constraints during generation—and generalize to continuous adaptation as tactical trends evolve—remains a core research challenge.

**Why It Matters:** Moving beyond static guidance modules is necessary for deploying generative models as reliable, longitudinal decision support systems in complex multi-agent environments.
