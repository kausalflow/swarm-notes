title: Convergence of Discrete HVNs
---

**Background:** The performance of Hilbert Covariance Networks (HVNs) relies on a discretization procedure applied to infinite-dimensional Hilbert spaces. The convergence properties of discrete HVFs/HVNs as the discretization resolution becomes infinitely fine-grained are not yet established.

**Question / Future Work:** Investigate the convergence behavior of discrete Hilbert Covariance Filters (HVFs) and Networks (HVNs) as the resolution of the signal discretization approaches the continuous limit. This is necessary to bridge the gap between finite-dimensional computational implementations and the continuous theoretical formulation.

**Why It Matters:** This is essential to guarantee the theoretical consistency and reliability of the framework across different discretization densities.
