title: Improving vector discharge prediction
---

**Background:** Predicting vector-valued flow discharges (velocity components) in hydrodynamic modeling is inherently more challenging than predicting scalar water depths due to the complex, non-linear behavior of fluxes near wet/dry interfaces and heterogeneous terrain.

**Question / Future Work:** The physical consistency of surrogate-predicted unit-width discharges (hu, hv) across varying hydraulic conditions and wet/dry boundaries remains an unresolved area of research essential for informing water management.

**Why It Matters:** Without accurate discharge predictions, flood models cannot fully inform structural risk assessments that depend on momentum and mass-flux conservation.
