title: Integrate data assimilation
---

**Background:** Machine learning weather models trained exclusively on historical reanalysis data may struggle to generalize to real-time conditions without adaptation.

**Question / Future Work:** Future work requires integrating data-assimilation techniques or developing multi-encoder schemes to effectively deploy the model for real-time operational forecasting, addressing the gap between training on historical reanalysis data and deployment with live observations.
