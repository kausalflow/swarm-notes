title: Integration of TDA and causal analysis
---

**Background:** Event detection and diagnosis in complex industrial processes often require both identifying the onset of an anomaly and understanding its underlying cause.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research aims to integrate topological event signatures with causal analysis and diagnosis tools to enable not only the detection of process instabilities but also the identification of the root cause or mechanism behind them.

**Why It Matters:** Identifying the root cause of an anomaly is essential for effective mitigation and control; merging topological detection with causal diagnosis would significantly improve the practical utility of process monitoring frameworks.
