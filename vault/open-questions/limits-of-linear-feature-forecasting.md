title: Non-linear Feature Forecasting Potential
---

**Background:** Diffusion models accelerate inference by caching and predicting feature trajectories, yet existing forecasting methods rely on linear approximations that may not fully capture non-linear feature evolution.

**Question / Future Work:** Future research should investigate whether non-linear modeling of feature dynamics in diffusion trajectories can yield significant gains in predictive accuracy or acceleration ratios without incurring prohibitive computational costs.

**Why It Matters:** This explores the theoretical ceiling of feature-caching acceleration and whether simple linear methods remain optimal as models scale in complexity.
