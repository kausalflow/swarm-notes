### Analytical Outlook
The most striking trend is the **de-siloing of time-series research.** Whether it is astronomers analyzing stellar pulsations (2604.21621), memristors classifying images through reservoir computing (2604.21602), or clinicians predicting mortality, the underlying concern is shifting toward **interpretability and physical consistency.**

Future research would benefit from focusing on the **Boundary-Profile Sensitivity** identified in streaming contexts, as many current "hybrid" successes may inadvertently be exploiting specific temporal partitioning rather than learning generalizable dynamics.
