# Universal Time-Series Representation Learning: A Survey

**Authors**: Patara Trirat, Yooju Shin, Jun-Hyeok Kang, Youngeun Nam, Jihye Na, Minyoung Bae, Jo‐Eun Kim, Byunghyun Kim, Jae-Gil Lee
**Date**: 2026-05-22
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2401.03717](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.03717)

## Summary

This paper provides a comprehensive survey of deep learning-based universal time-series representation learning. It introduces a novel taxonomy structured around three core design components to categorize existing methodologies and analyze their underlying intuitions. By synthesizing current research, the survey offers valuable insights into representation quality and establishes a framework for evaluating future studies through standardized experimental setups.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a novel taxonomy for universal time-series representation learning methods based on three fundamental design elements.
- Performs a comprehensive review of existing deep learning-based representation learning studies, offering insights into how design choices affect representation quality.
- Summarizes experimental setups and standard datasets to serve as a guideline for future research.
- Identifies and discusses promising open research directions for the field.
