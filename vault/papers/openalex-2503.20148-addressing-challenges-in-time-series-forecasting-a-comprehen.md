# Addressing challenges in time series forecasting: a comprehensive comparison of machine learning techniques

**Authors**: Seyedeh Azadeh Fallah Mortezanejad, Ruochen Wang
**Date**: 2026-05-18
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2503.20148](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.20148)

## Summary

This paper provides a comparative study of machine learning algorithms for time series forecasting, focusing on their effectiveness in handling complex data conditions such as outliers and missing values. The authors benchmark these modern ML approaches against the classical ARIMA model to determine their suitability for long-term prediction tasks. The findings offer a practical guide for researchers and practitioners to select appropriate forecasting models based on specific data characteristics and requirements.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a comparative analysis of state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms for time series regression.
- Evaluates forecasting performance against the classic ARIMA method under varying data conditions including outliers and missing values.
- Assesses model robustness and accuracy specifically for long-term time series forecasting scenarios.
