# From Forecasts to Auditable Reports: Evidence Contracts for LLM-Assisted Housing-Guarantee Risk Monitoring

**Authors**: Hyeongcheol Kim, Y. Hwang
**Date**: 2026-07-15
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2607.14026](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14026)

## Summary

This paper presents an evidence-constrained reporting pipeline designed to transform housing-guarantee risk forecasts into auditable operational reports. By leveraging typed evidence contracts, the framework improves the numerical fidelity and claim-level grounding of LLM-generated narratives, specifically for monitoring sparse upper-tail events. Practitioner evaluations with domain experts demonstrate that this approach effectively supports real-world decision-making and human oversight.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces an evidence-constrained reporting pipeline that links high-risk forecasts to auditable reports using typed evidence contracts.
- Demonstrates that structured evidence significantly improves numerical fidelity and claim-level grounding across various LLMs.
- Validates the utility of the framework through a practitioner evaluation with 51 analysts, showing improved support for real-world decision-making.
