# The Northern Cross Fast Radio Burst project: VI. The INCART public database

**Authors**: L. Bruno, G. Bernardi, M. Pilia, D. Pelliciari, A. Geminardi, F. Fiori, V. Galluzzi, G. Naldi, M. Trudu, A. Zanichelli
**Date**: 2026-05-28
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2605.30192](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30192)

## Summary

This paper presents the Italian Northern Cross Atlas of Radio Transients (INCART), a new public platform for accessing and analyzing Fast Radio Burst (FRB) data collected by the Northern Cross radio interferometer. INCART serves as a repository for frequency-time series datasets and best-fit physical parameters, ensuring that observational data is readily available for scientific re-analysis. By focusing on long-term storage optimization, the project maximizes the legacy value of Northern Cross observations and promotes collaborative research within the astrophysical community.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces INCART, a public platform for the distribution and scientific re-analysis of Fast Radio Burst (FRB) data from the Northern Cross radio interferometer.
- Provides a framework for long-term data storage optimization specifically designed for state-of-the-art radio transient instrumentation.
- Facilitates community-wide collaborative research and synergy between NC data and other existing astronomical FRB catalogues.
