## Summary

DigiForest is a comprehensive precision forestry framework that addresses the need for sustainable forest management through robotics and digital analytics. The approach integrates heterogeneous autonomous robots—including aerial, legged, and marsupial platforms—to perform high-resolution, tree-level data collection. This data powers an automated inventory system and a decision support system (DSS) for growth forecasting, ultimately facilitating low-impact, autonomous selective logging. The framework has been validated through extensive field deployments across diverse European forest ecosystems.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces DigiForest, an end-to-end precision forestry framework integrating autonomous aerial, legged, and marsupial robotics for tree-level data collection.
- Develops automated pipelines for extracting fine-grained tree traits to construct comprehensive, scalable forest inventories.
- Deploys a Decision Support System (DSS) for forest growth forecasting and implements low-impact selective logging through autonomous harvester platforms.
