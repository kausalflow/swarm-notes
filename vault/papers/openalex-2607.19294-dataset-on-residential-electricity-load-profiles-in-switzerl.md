# Dataset on residential electricity load profiles in Switzerland

**Authors**: Katharina Kaiser, Markus Kreft, Marina González Vayá, Gabriela Hug
**Date**: 2026-07-21
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2607.19294](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19294)

## Summary

This paper introduces a real-world smart meter dataset comprising 15-minute resolution electricity load profiles from 2,447 residential installations in Switzerland for the years 2023 and 2024. The dataset covers active and reactive energy withdrawal and injection across diverse load types like heat pumps and apartments, alongside metadata and price profiles from a pilot project involving novel grid tariffs and automated load control. This empirical resource is designed to support research in distribution grid modeling, load forecasting, and dynamic tariff evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Presents a comprehensive dataset of 15-minute smart meter measurements from 2,447 residential installations in Switzerland covering 2023 and 2024.
- Includes active and reactive energy data (withdrawal and injection) across diverse installation types such as heat pumps, electric vehicles, and apartments.
- Provides companion metadata, price profiles, and pilot project records featuring novel grid tariffs and automated load control.
