# ProPACT: A Proactive AI-Driven Adaptive Collaborative Tutor for Pair Programming

**Authors**: Anahita Golrang, Kshitij Sharma, olga viberg
**Date**: 2026-05-04
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2605.02703](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02703)

## Summary

ProPACT is a proactive AI-driven tutor that models and regulates collaboration during pair programming by focusing on joint visual attention and mental effort. Unlike reactive individual-centric systems, it uses an XGBoost-based forecasting model to predict and intervene upon suboptimal collaboration states up to 30 seconds before they occur. Empirical validation with 26 dyads confirms that this forecast-driven approach improves both task efficiency and long-term joint engagement metrics compared to baseline collaborative conditions.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces ProPACT, a proactive AI-driven system for real-time regulation of pair programming through multimodal dyadic modeling.
- Implements a predictive forecasting model using XGBoost to identify suboptimal collaboration states up to 30 seconds ahead.
- Demonstrates through a study with 26 dyads that proactive scaffolds significantly enhance debugging performance and joint cognitive engagement (JVA/JME).
