# Asymptotic behavior of spatio-temporal point processes of exceedances

**Authors**: Carolin Forster, Marco Oesting
**Date**: 2026-04-13
**Paper ID**: [openalex:2604.11691](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11691)

## Summary

This paper examines the asymptotic distribution of spatio-temporal exceedances defined through site-dependent risk functionals. By utilizing the framework of stationary regularly varying multivariate time series, the authors demonstrate the weak convergence of point processes representing rescaled occurrence times, locations, and exceedance magnitudes. The work provides a rigorous derivation of the limit distribution, offering a theoretical foundation for modeling extremes in spatio-temporal data.

## Key Contributions

- Establishes the weak convergence of spatio-temporal point processes of exceedances using rescaled occurrence times, sites, and exceedance values.
- Provides an explicit characterization of the limit distribution for extremes defined via flexible, site-dependent risk functionals.
- Extends extreme value theory frameworks to accommodate stationary regularly varying multivariate time series in a continuous spatio-temporal context.
