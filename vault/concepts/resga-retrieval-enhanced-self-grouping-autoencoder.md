# Retrieval-Enhanced Self-Grouping Autoencoder (ReSGA)

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A large-scale autoencoder architecture that leverages retrieval and self-grouping to capture cross-sectional and temporal risk dynamics.


## Why It Matters

It is the central architecture introduced for jointly forecasting Value-at-Risk (VaR) and Expected Shortfall (ES).

## Evidence

> we propose a large tail risk model, the retrieval-enhanced self-grouping autoencoder (ReSGA), which is designed with millions of parameters to exploit the rich cross-sectional dependence and long-term temporal dynamics of assets using their characteristics.
