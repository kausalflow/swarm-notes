# DLNM-Clust

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A mixture model for environmental epidemiology that assigns geographic units to latent clusters, each with a distinct distributed lag non-linear model specification.


## Why It Matters

It addresses the limitation of homogeneous exposure-lag-response associations in traditional DLNMs by allowing spatial heterogeneity through latent cluster assignment.

## Evidence

> we introduce DLNM‐Clust: a novel mixture of DLNMs that extends the traditional DLNM. Within a Bayesian framework, DLNM‐Clust probabilistically assigns each geographic unit to one of latent spatial clusters
