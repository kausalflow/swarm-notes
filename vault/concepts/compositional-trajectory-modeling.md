# Compositional Trajectory Modeling

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A framework for representing trajectories as proportions of road types within a simplex to account for measurement uncertainty.


## Why It Matters

Provides a novel way to encode spatial and categorical uncertainty in mobility data by mapping observations onto the simplex, enabling state-space modeling.

## Evidence

> We introduce a compositional representation of individual movements that integrates the uncertain device location with information on the surrounding road network, encoding at each time point the proportions of different road types compatible with the observed position.
