# Decision-Focused Forecasting Framework

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A training paradigm that integrates downstream multi-stage optimization objectives into upstream forecasting models to mitigate cascading forecast error impacts.


## Why It Matters

It addresses the critical problem of cascading errors in hierarchical control systems by aligning forecasting models directly with downstream decision objectives rather than proxy statistical metrics.

## Evidence

> By integrating the downstream multi-stage VVC optimization into the upstream forecasting models training, the decision-focused forecasting models are able to learn the trade-offs across temporal horizons.
