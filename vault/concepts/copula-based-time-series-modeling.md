# Copula-Based Time Series Modeling

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A framework for modeling univariate time series dependence by capturing finite-dimensional joint distributions via copulas.


## Why It Matters

Provides a flexible, non-parametric framework for modeling complex dependencies in non-Gaussian, non-Markovian stationary processes.

## Evidence

> In the copula-based approach to univariate time series modeling, the finite dimensional temporal dependence of a stationary time series is captured by a copula.
