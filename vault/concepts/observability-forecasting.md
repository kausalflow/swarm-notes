# Observability Forecasting

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A predictive task focusing on determining whether the surface is visible in future Earth Observation acquisitions rather than predicting surface values directly.


## Why It Matters

Shifts the focus from surface state prediction to the binary usability of Earth Observation imagery, which is a critical operational bottleneck.
