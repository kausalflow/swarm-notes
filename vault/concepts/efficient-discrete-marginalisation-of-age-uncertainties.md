# Efficient discrete marginalisation of age uncertainties

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A technique for integrating chronological uncertainty into Bayesian models by marginalizing over temporal indices instead of co-estimating them.


## Why It Matters

Addresses the fundamental challenge of high-dimensional MCMC convergence when dealing with imprecise chronological data in geophysical inversions.

## Evidence

> A key innovation is an efficient discrete marginalisation of age uncertainties, which avoids the convergence difficulties associated with co-estimating ages in high-dimensional Hamiltonian Monte Carlo inversions.
