# Generalized Lagrangian Mean (GLM) Filtering

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A filtering framework for decomposing Lagrangian velocities into physically meaningful wave and mean flow components by attributing velocities to mean rather than particle positions.


## Why It Matters

Provides a physically rigorous foundation for separating wave and mean flow components in Lagrangian trajectories, correcting for the limitations of standard Helmholtz decomposition in dynamic settings.

## Evidence

> A key methodological choice is to implement the filtering in a generalized Lagrangian mean (GLM) framework, attributing filtered velocities to mean rather than particle trajectories
