# Empirical Passband Contrast-Contrast Relationship

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A power-law relationship derived from high-resolution observations to map solar contrast measurements across different spectral passbands and spatial resolutions.


## Why It Matters

This relationship is the core novelty that allows for cross-calibration of disparate solar observation archives, essential for constructing long-term solar activity records.

## Evidence

> we use high spectral and spatial resolution observations in the Ca II K line from the state-of-the-art Sunrise III mission to emulate different passbands and derive empirical contrast-contrast relationships between them.
