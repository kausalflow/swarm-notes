# Golden Criterion

> *Auto-generated stub. Edit this file to add more details.*

A theoretical condition for the optimality of equal-weighted portfolios based on the uniformity of the forecast-error covariance matrix eigenstructure.


## Why It Matters

It provides a theoretical foundation for understanding when and why equal-weighted (naïve) diversification outperforms optimized weight strategies.

## Evidence

> equal weighting is minimum-variance optimal when the forecast-error covariance matrix has a uniform eigenstructure. This \"Golden Criterion\" drives a two-stage adaptive strategy
